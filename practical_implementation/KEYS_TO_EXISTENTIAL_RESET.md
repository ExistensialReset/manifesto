## A

### AI Mirror
A human role (not AI itself) that monitors AI systems for errors, bias, or harm. Has authority to override AI decisions. See *Mirror*, *Symbiotic Intelligence*.

### AI-Fast
Periodic shutdown of AI systems to prevent overreliance and maintain human skill retention. Ensures humans can function without AI during crisis.

### Anchor (in Dual-Memory)
Immutable rules in AI memory that cannot be changed by learning or adaptation. Includes: Baseline protection, non-harm principles, human veto rights. See *Compost*, *Dual-Memory*.

### Anti-Capture Protocol
Safeguards preventing Flow from being co-opted by extractive systems (capitalism, authoritarianism, tech corporations). Includes: no stock issuance, no venture capital, mandatory transparency.

### Axiom
Fundamental, non-negotiable principle. The system has three core Axioms:
1. **Axiom 1 (Non-Coercion):** Participation is voluntary; no one can be forced
2. **Axiom 2 (Cognitive Ownership):** Each person's attention and thought are their absolute property
3. **Axiom 3 (Baseline Primacy):** Baseline cannot be violated under any circumstances

See *Baseline*.

## B

### Baseline
**The unconditional guarantee of survival needs:**
- Clean water (50L/person/day minimum)
- Nutritious food
- Shelter with thermal comfort (~20°C)
- Healthcare access
- Energy for basic needs
- Internet connectivity (information access)

**Critical:** Baseline is **never conditional**. Not tied to productivity, contribution, behavior, or identity. Exists simply because you exist.

See *Baseline Emergency Bypass*, *Mammonology*.

### Baseline Emergency Bypass
Rare protocol allowing temporary suspension of normal decision-making processes **only** to protect Baseline during existential crisis (e.g., natural disaster, violent attack). Cannot be used for convenience or optimization. Requires documentation and review within 72 hours.

### Buffer
Resources beyond Baseline. Optional, varied, creative. Can be differentiated (richer Nodes can have "nicer" Buffer), but Baseline must be equivalent across all Nodes.

## C

### Circle
Small-scale community unit (typically 20-150 people) living in Flow principles. Has internal self-governance, shares resources, coordinates locally. Multiple Circles form a *Node* or *Regional Network*.

### Cognitive Ownership
See *Axiom 2*. The principle that your attention, thoughts, and mental processes belong to you alone. No one can demand your focus, surveil your thinking, or monetize your attention without consent.

### Compost (in Dual-Memory)
Adaptive memory in AI systems that learns from experience and updates. Cannot override *Anchor* rules. Allows AI to improve while maintaining ethical boundaries.

### Contribution
In Flow, any action that adds value to life:
- Physical labor (building, gardening, cooking)
- Emotional labor (listening, care, presence)
- Intellectual labor (teaching, problem-solving, documentation)
- Cultural labor (art, music, storytelling, ritual)
- Survival labor (managing chronic illness, navigating systems)

**Critically:** Contribution is **optional**, not required for Baseline access. See *Free Rider Question*.

### Cult Risk
The danger that Flow communities could become closed, coercive systems with charismatic leaders. Safeguards include: *Founder Irrelevance*, external *Ombudsperson*, *Child Veto*, right to exit without penalty.

## D

### DIVINE
**Formula:** Ψ = [EV ⊗ (L × S × I × K × R × F)] + Σ

A framework for understanding human flourishing that cannot be reduced to metrics. Components:
- **Ψ (Psi):** Flow state; aliveness
- **EV:** Ecological Wisdom (millions of years of evolutionary knowledge)
- **L:** Lugn (Calm) - nervous system safety
- **S:** Spontanitet (Spontaneity) - irreducible freedom
- **I:** Inkännande (Empathy) - felt connection to others
- **K:** Kollektivt medvetande (Collective Consciousness)
- **R:** Resiliens (Resilience)
- **F:** Förundran (Wonder/Awe)
- **Σ (Sigma):** Spjuver/Grace - spontaneous emergence beyond prediction

**Warning:** DIVINE is **diagnostic**, not prescriptive. It explains what humans need to flourish but **must never be used for ranking, KPIs, gamification, or optimization**. See *DIVINE-MEASURE*, *HOW-NOT-TO*.

### DIVINE-MEASURE
Guidelines for how (and how not to) measure Ψ. Emphasizes:
- Qualitative over quantitative
- Self-reflection over external measurement
- Voluntary participation
- No dashboards, no leaderboards, no social credit

If measurement feels like surveillance → stop immediately.

### Dual-Memory
AI architecture with two memory systems:
1. **Anchor:** Immutable ethical rules
2. **Compost:** Adaptive learning

Prevents AI from "learning away" its ethical constraints while allowing improvement.

## E

### Ecological Wisdom (EV)
The deep intelligence embedded in evolutionary biology, ecosystems, and natural processes over millions of years. Flow respects this by:
- 30% Wild-Mandate (leaving land untouched)
- Regenerative Time Constant (*RTC*) respect
- Reciprocity over extraction

### Elite Capture
Risk that wealthy Nodes use resources to dominate others through better AI, infrastructure, or influence. Prevented by: *Solidarity Fund*, equivalent *Baseline AI* across Nodes, knowledge commons, network architecture preventing hub dominance.

### Exit Protocol
Formal process for leaving a Circle without penalty, shame, or loss of dignity. Includes:
- Right to leave at any time
- Baseline support during transition
- Assistance returning to "Mammon world" if needed
- No "betrayal" narrative

Particularly important for children (see *Child Veto*).

### Existential Reset
Alternative name for the entire M-OS-R/Flow system. Emphasizes the project's purpose: resetting humanity's relationship with existence itself.

## F

### Flow
**Three meanings:**
1. **Psychological state** (Ψ): Being fully alive, present, engaged
2. **System name:** The social/economic alternative to Mammon
3. **Movement:** Living in accordance with Life (L × S × I)

Not to be confused with Mihály Csíkszentmihályi's "flow state" (though related).

### Flow-Circle
See *Circle*.

### Flow ID
Anonymous identifier for participants in Flow networks. Protects privacy while allowing coordination. Can be erased annually (except essential medical records).

### Founder Irrelevance
Core principle: The system must survive without its creator(s). Everything must be documented so outsiders can operate Nodes independently. Elinor Frejd explicitly does not want to lead or be necessary.

### Förundran (F in DIVINE)
**Swedish:** Wonder/Awe  
**In DIVINE:** The capacity to be amazed, to experience beauty and mystery without needing to control or understand it. Childlike openness to the world. The opposite of cynicism and extraction.

### Free Rider Question
"What if people take Baseline and never contribute?"

**Flow's answer:**
1. Most humans naturally want to contribute when survival isn't coerced
2. Some genuinely cannot contribute (severe disability, illness) - they still get Baseline
3. Some will take advantage (~5%) - this is acceptable cost of ensuring no one starves
4. Social dynamics (not coercion) may emerge: gentle inquiry after extended non-participation, invitation to find meaningful contribution

See *Contribution*.

## H

### Harm Boundary Protocol
Process for handling interpersonal harm within Circles:
- Victim never moves; alleged perpetrator is separated
- Requires clear and convincing evidence for permanent action
- 14-day review period for full investigation
- Independent panel (lottery-selected)
- Baseline guaranteed even to accused

See *Unforgivable Harm Protocol* for emergency response version.

### HOW-NOT-TO
Document listing prohibited uses of DIVINE/Flow:
- Never use for ranking humans
- Never gamify Ψ
- Never create KPIs or dashboards
- Never deny Baseline based on measurements
- Never surveil without consent

## L

### L (Lugn)
**Swedish:** Calm  
**In DIVINE:** Foundational nervous system safety. The first requirement for flourishing.

Without L: S (spontaneity) becomes chaos; I (empathy) becomes overwhelm.

One of three core factors: **L × S × I = Life**

### Lex Custodia
Emergency protocol triggered when climate threats endanger Baseline (e.g., extreme heat, water scarcity). Allows collective relocation or emergency resource mobilization.

### Life Formula
**L × S × I**
- **L (Lugn):** Calm
- **S (Spontanitet):** Spontaneity
- **I (Inkännande):** Empathy

Multiplicative (not additive): if any factor is zero, life quality collapses.

### LOTUS Protocol
**"Lottery-based Organization of Temporary Unstable Seats"**

Selection method for cross-Node roles:
- Deterministic lottery (auditable, offline-verifiable)
- Maximum 9 months consecutive service
- Maximum 2 selections per year
- Prevents representative power concentration
- No campaigning, no permanent governance class

### Lyceum Musaeum
Physical/conceptual space for learning, curiosity, play, and creation. Free access to:
- Tools and workshops
- Knowledge and teaching
- Materials for exploration
- Time and space for mastery

Named after Aristotle's Lyceum, but without elitism.

## M

### Mammon
**Symbolic name for extraction-based systems:**
- Capitalism requiring infinite growth
- Debt as control mechanism
- Commodification of attention, time, bodies
- Artificial scarcity despite abundance
- Productivity as moral worth

Not used as insult, but as diagnosis of systemic patterns.

### Mammonology
Study of how Mammon-systems function and how to exit them without replicating their logic. Includes analysis of:
- Attention extraction
- Debt coercion
- Status anxiety
- Manufactured scarcity

### Mandate of Nine Moons
Last-resort collective decision protocol for existential deadlock within Circle. Used when:
- All other conflict resolution methods have failed
- Issue threatens Circle survival
- Deadlock persists for extended period

**Critical safeguard:** **Child Veto**. If a child objects, protocol stops and system goes to "Structured Coexistence" instead. Principle: No decision is worth traumatizing a child.

Can only be used once in a person's lifetime. Requires 3-month decompression after service.

### Mirror
Human role monitoring systems (AI, resource allocation, governance) for errors, bias, or harm. Has override authority. Multiple types:
- AI Mirrors (watch AI systems)
- Resource Mirrors (watch distribution)
- Protocol Mirrors (watch governance)

Not authority figures - they observe and intervene only when systems drift from principles.

### M-OS-R
**"Manifesto-Operating System-Reset"**

Technical name for the entire system. Emphasizes three layers:
1. **Manifesto:** Ethical foundation
2. **Operating System:** Practical protocols
3. **Reset:** Existential recalibration of human purpose

Often referred to simply as "Flow" or "Existential Reset".

## N

### Neurodivergence
Cognitive/neurological variations (autism, ADHD, etc.). Flow treats as **variation**, not deficit:
- Accommodations are default, not special
- Stimming/fidgeting is acceptable
- Communication flexibility (written, verbal, processing time)
- No forced eye contact or neurotypical performance

### Node
Organizational unit larger than a Circle, smaller than a region. Typically coordinates multiple Circles, shares infrastructure (energy, water, healthcare), maintains documentation.

### Non-Harm Baseline
Technical document defining minimum acceptable operational behavior for any system (human or AI):
- Must not deplete resources faster than regeneration
- Must not operate faster than slowest dependent system
- Must be able to stop/degrade safely
- Must account for all outputs and recovery paths

See *RTC*, *Silence Rule*.

## O

### Ombudsperson
External advocate for children in Flow communities. Not chosen by Circle - chosen by child and external network. Meets privately with child quarterly. If Circle refuses Ombudsperson access → loses Flow recognition.

Prevents cult dynamics by ensuring external oversight.

## P

### Precautionary Principle
When uncertain of harm potential → default to inaction or human supervision. "First, do no harm" applied to systems design.

### Ψ (Psi)
Greek letter representing Flow state / aliveness / human flourishing in DIVINE equation. Diagnostic measure, never prescriptive. Cannot be optimized directly - attempting to do so destroys it.

## R

### Reciprocity
Core ecological principle: We don't "take" from nature - we trade. Human waste (compost, CO₂ capture, care) becomes raw material for ecosystems. Move from parasitic to symbiotic existence.

### Refugium Anima
**"Sanctuary of Soul"**

Protected space for:
- Rest without productivity
- Nervous system repair
- Grief processing
- Recovery from trauma
- Doing absolutely nothing

No one can be forced out of Refugium. Even chronic non-contribution doesn't forfeit access.

### Regenerative Time Constant (RTC)
Time required for a natural system to heal itself after extraction/use. Examples:
- Soil regeneration: years to decades
- Forest regrowth: decades to centuries
- Aquifer recharge: varies by geology

**Rule:** Human activity must not exceed regenerative capacity. If RTC unknown → pause or reduce throughput.

### Regional Network
Coordination layer between Nodes. Facilitates:
- Resource exchange (15% regional, 5% global)
- Knowledge sharing
- Conflict mediation
- Emergency mutual aid

No central authority - voluntary coordination.

### Resiliens (R in DIVINE)
**Swedish:** Resilience  
**In DIVINE:** The capacity to bend without breaking, to recover from difficulty, to adapt while maintaining core integrity. Both individual and collective ability to weather storms.

## S

### S (Spontanitet)
**Swedish:** Spontaneity  
**In DIVINE:** Irreducible freedom. The capacity to act from authentic impulse, not coercion or programming.

One of three core factors: L × S × **I**

### Σ (Sigma / Spjuver)
**"Grace" or "Spontaneous field fluctuations"**

The unpredictable, emergent quality that cannot be controlled or optimized. Examples:
- Unexpected joy
- Synchronicity
- Moments of beauty
- Grace itself

In DIVINE equation: added (not multiplied), because it arrives regardless of other factors.

**Critical:** Any attempt to engineer Σ destroys it. It must be allowed, not pursued.

### Silence Rule
**For AI and automated systems:** If a system cannot determine whether harm is occurring → correct behavior is **silence** (no action, no optimization, no recommendation).

Silence is considered safer than incorrect action.

### Solidarity Fund
Temporary mechanism (10-20 years) to prevent wealth inequality from becoming AI/infrastructure dominance:
- Wealthy Nodes contribute 5-10% of startup assets
- Poor Nodes receive hardware, training, mentorship
- Goal: By Year 20, cannot distinguish historically rich vs poor Nodes

See *Elite Capture*, *Wealth Inequality Dampening*.

### Sovereignty
Multilayered concept:
1. **Bodily sovereignty:** Right to refuse medical treatment, choose appearance, control own body
2. **Cognitive sovereignty:** Your attention belongs to you (*Axiom 2*)
3. **Temporal sovereignty:** Your time is yours, not sold by hour
4. **Ecological sovereignty:** Land's right to self-organize (30% Wild-Mandate)

### Spiritual Bypassing
Using Flow concepts to **avoid** necessary political/economic action. Examples:
- "Just raise your Ψ" → while ignoring structural oppression
- "Be grateful" → while people lack Baseline
- Optimizing inner peace → instead of confronting injustice

DIVINE §9 explicitly warns against this. Flow is **not** a replacement for material justice.

### Spjuver
See *Sigma (Σ)*. Swedish term for spontaneous emergence, grace, the unpredictable blessing.

### Structural Coexistence
Outcome when *Mandate of Nine Moons* is vetoed by a child. Circle acknowledges deadlock but chooses **not to force resolution**. Parties agree to coexist with unresolved tension rather than traumatize child with forced decision.

### Symbiotic Intelligence
AI architecture where:
- AI is companion, not controller
- Humans retain veto power always
- AI must degrade/silence when uncertain
- No "emergency exception" logic allowing AI autonomy escalation

See *Dual-Memory*, *AI Mirror*, *Silence Rule*.

### Veto Rights
Multiple types:
- **Human veto over AI:** Always. No exceptions.
- **Child veto:** Children can stop *Mandate of Nine Moons* → triggers *Structured Coexistence*

Veto power is **absolute** in defined domains. Cannot be overridden "for the greater good".
