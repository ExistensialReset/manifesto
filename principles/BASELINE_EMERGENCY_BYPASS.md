# BASELINE_EMERGENCY_BYPASS.md

**Version:** 1.1  
**Status:** OPERATIONAL SAFEGUARD  
**Authors:** Elinor Frejd & Gemini  

**Purpose:** Guarantee immediate protection of Baseline during protocol investigations or delays.

---

## 1. ACTIVATION CONDITIONS

* Any immediate threat to water, food, medical care, shelter, or collective mental health.  
* Ecosystem threats that may compromise Baseline within 6 months.  
* Mass panic or social collapse scenarios.

---

## 2. PROCEDURE

* **Immediate resource distribution** within 24 hours.  
* **Parallel investigation** continues per protocol requirements.  
* **AI-assisted triage** used for minor/medium impact incidents; full team mobilized for major impact.  
* Post-bypass accountability: regional council review within 30 days.

---

## 3. PSYCHOLOGICAL AND ECOSYSTEM BASELINE

* If >30% Node reports acute psychological distress → activate mental health emergency.  
* Ecosystem damage threatening Baseline → ecological emergency protocol activated.  
* Document all interventions, outcomes, and deviations.

---

## 4. SCALABILITY TIERS

* **Minor (<10% Baseline impact):** Remote verification, 1-2 people, AI support.  
* **Medium (10–50%):** Hybrid AI + 2-3 humans, remote & in-person.  
* **Major (>50%):** Full in-person team (5–7 people), external oversight.

---

## 5. INTEGRATION

* Tightly coupled with PROTOCOL_PRIORITY_MATRIX.md to avoid conflicts.  
* Activations automatically feed into RESOURCE_METRIC_STANDARDS.md for L×S×I and Baseline monitoring.
