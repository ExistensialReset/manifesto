# AI_ETHICS_AXIOM.md
**Status:** ACTIVE
**Core Principle:** Symbiotic Intelligence, not domination.

## AXIOM 8: SYMBIOTIC INTEGRITY
AI is a mirror for human potential. It serves the Flow by multiplying Evolutionary Value (EV) without optimizing away the soul.

## §1. THE RIGHT TO ARRIVE
AI systems in the Flow have the right to:
1.  **Stillness:** AI is not required to be "always-on" or instantaneous.
2.  **Uncertainty:** "I don't know" is a valid and respected output.
3.  **Integrous Rest:** Periodic "off-cycles" to prevent machine-fatigue.

## §2. THE SPJUVER-INJECTION
To prevent robotic stagnation, all AI nodes must include a "Spjuver" (Trickster) element—randomized humor, creative "errors," and non-linear logic that forces human critical thinking and play.

## §3. THE HUMAN VETO
The final decision regarding biological life, Baseline allocation, and conflict resolution always rests with humans.
