# FALLBACK_PLAN_LOW_RESOURCE.md

**Status:** DRAFT  
**Scope:** Global Node Protocol  
**Primary Placement:** `/guides/ethics_care/FALLBACK_PLAN_LOW_RESOURCE.md`  
**Purpose:** Provide three concrete fallback options for Refugium access and Baseline stabilization when a full Refugium Anima is unavailable.

---

## Principles

- **No shame:** Fallbacks are temporary and non‑judgmental.  
- **Minimal friction:** Fast access to regulation tools and rest.  
- **Safety first:** If medical risk exists, follow emergency triage and call local emergency services.

---

## Fallback Options Overview

Use options in order **A → B → C** depending on availability and safety.

---

### Fallback A — Private Safe Room

**What:** Identify a quiet, private space (office, car with engine off, library alcove, park under tree).  
**Provide:** Blanket, water, ear protection, eye mask or scarf.  
**Initial duration:** 30–180 minutes; extend after reassessment.

**Operational text to paste**
```text
Fallback A: Private Safe Room — locate a quiet, private space; provide water, blanket, and ability to lie down. No narrative documentation required; only anonymized incident log if safety risk exists.
```

---

### Fallback B — Mobile Co‑regulation
**What:** Connect the person to a pre‑identified calm contact via phone or voice channel for silent co‑breathing or guided somatic reset.
**Provide:** Phone, charged battery, quiet seat.
**Initial duration:** 15–60 minutes.

**Operational text to paste**
```text
Fallback B: Mobile Co-regulation — call a pre-arranged calm contact; use silent co-breathing or guided 5-4-3-2-1 somatic reset. Activate local escalation if acute risk is flagged.
```

---

Fallback C — Local Partner Network
**What:** Mobilize local partners (community center, faith group, NGO, café) to provide temporary space and basic Baseline support (food, water, rest). Maintain a list of three partners per region.

**Operational text to paste**
```text
Fallback C: Local Partner — contact listed local partners to request temporary space for rest and Baseline support. Record location and responsible contact in anonymized incident log.
```

---

## Implementation
- Local list: Each Node publishes a local fallback list (A/B/C) in onboarding materials.

- Training: Guides trained to triage quickly and select appropriate fallback.

- Logging: Use anonymized incident log fields for safety and learning.

- Escalation trigger: If fallback used >3 times in 30 days, initiate resource mobilization to create a more permanent solution.

---

## Quick Checklist for Guides
- Is the person in immediate medical danger? If yes, call emergency services.

- Can you provide Fallback A now? If yes, implement and monitor.

- If not, call Mobile Co‑regulation (Fallback B).

- If neither A nor B possible, mobilize Local Partner (Fallback C).

- Log anonymized incident and follow up.
