## PART I: ADAPTATIONS FOR AUTISTIC FOLKS

### What Autistic Brains Often Need:

**1. Predictability**

**What helps:**
- Fixed meeting times (same day, same time every week)
- Shared agenda sent 24 hours ahead
- Clear start/end times (and stick to them)
- Explicit social rules (written down)
