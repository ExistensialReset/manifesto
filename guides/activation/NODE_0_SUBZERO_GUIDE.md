## 1. DEFINITION OF SUB-ZERO
You are in Sub-Zero when the physical Baseline is broken. You have no reliable access to water, heat, or safety. You own nothing but your body and your voice.

---

## 2. PROTOCOL 0: THE INTERNAL BASELINE
When the external world is stolen, we move the Baseline to that which cannot be reached from the outside.

* **Breath as Territory:** Every conscious breath is an occupation of space. You proclaim: *"I take up space. I still exist."*
* **The Heat Anchor:** Find a point on your body that is warmer than the rest (the inside of a hand, the back of the neck). Focus all attention there. This is your smallest possible Node.

---

## 3. THE L-FACTOR: REGULATION IN CHAOS (Calm)
* **Somatic Anchoring:** Feel the pressure of your feet against the ground. This is your contact with the planet. The Earth feels your feet even if they are bare and cold.
* **Biological Solidarity:** Your body is doing its job to keep you alive under terrible circumstances. Refusing to despise your suffering body is a victory for **L (Calm)**.

---

## 4. THE S-FACTOR: SONIC RESISTANCE (Spontaneity)
Spontaneity is the spark that makes you a human instead of a machine.

* **Burst into Song:** If you can, sing. If you must be silent for safety, hum inside your own chest so only your own body vibrates. Singing is not entertainment; it is a logistical occupation of the air. It is a declaration of life.
* **The Absurd Movement:** Perform one movement that has nothing to do with survival. Wiggle a toe in a rhythm only you know. This is the proof that your **S (Spontaneity)** is not crushed.

---

## 5. THE I-FACTOR: RESONANCE IN THE RUINS (Empathy)
* **Radical Visibility:** Look at another human being and acknowledge their existence without wanting anything. A silent recognition: *"I see you. We are here together."*
* **Breaking the Logic of Competition:** If you have a crumb and you share it, you have shattered the foundation of Mammon-logic. You have created a Circle out of nothing.

---

## 6. LOGISTICAL GRAVITATION FROM ZERO
* **Observation:** Where are the flows? Where are the scraps? Who has excess? Knowledge of flows is your first resource accumulation.
* **Dignity-Exit:** If the system demands your last shred of dignity in exchange for survival: Choose the "No." Maintaining sovereignty in an internal "No" is the ultimate victory over extraction.
