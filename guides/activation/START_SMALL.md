## PART II: THE MICRO-CIRCLE GUIDE (2-5 People, 12 Weeks)

**What this is:**  
A practical protocol for 2-5 people who want to try Flow together.

**Time commitment:**  
1 meeting per week (1-2 hours).

**Goal:**  
By Week 12, you have a living Micro-Circle â€“ not perfect, but real.

---

### WEEK 1: Mapping (No Fixing)

**What to do:**

Each person shares:

**1. My Mammon-Count**  
How many fears per day? (from Day 1 exercise)

**2. My Real Baseline**  
What do I actually need to survive?
- Rent: [amount]
- Food: [amount]
- Healthcare: [amount]
- Transport: [amount]
- **Total: [amount]**

**3. My Parasitic Cost**

- Interest on debt: [amount]
- Subscriptions I don't use: [amount]
- Stress-buying: [amount]
- Commute to job I hate: [amount]
- **Total waste: [amount]**

**No one fixes.**  
**Just see.**

Write it down.

---

### WEEK 2: Share One Thing

**What to do:**

Each person shares something they have that someone else needs.

**Examples:**
- Tools (drill, ladder, sewing machine)
- Knowledge (how to fix something, how to cook something)
- Time (help move, help clean, help think)
- Food (extra vegetables, bulk-bought rice)

**Not formally.**  
**Just test.**

Document:
- What was shared?
- Did it work?
- What did we learn?

---

### WEEK 3: Measure Change

**What to do:**

Each person answers:

**1. Do I feel less afraid?**  
(Compare Week 1 Mammon-Count with now)

**2. Have I saved money through sharing?**  
(Even 50 kr matters)

**3. Do I feel less alone?**  
(Yes/No/Sometimes)

**If yes to any: continue.**  
**If no to all: adjust or pause.**

### How To Apply This Daily

**Before you buy something, ask:**

4. Do I actually need this, or does Mammon tell me I do?

---

**When you need help, ask:**

3. Can I receive without owing?

**If you ask and receive: PLUS.**

---

**When someone needs help, ask:**

1. Can I give without expecting return?

**If you give freely: PLUS.**
