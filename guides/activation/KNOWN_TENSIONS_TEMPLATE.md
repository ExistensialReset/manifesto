## FIELD INSTRUCTIONS

1. **Tension:** Copy from `KNOWN_TENSIONS.md` or describe new emergent tension  
2. **Date Encountered:** When the friction was observed  
3. **Node / Circle:** Name or identifier  
4. **Observed Impact:** Rate effect on Calm, Spontaneity, Empathy, and Baseline  
5. **Action Taken:** Describe steps, interventions, or experiments  
6. **Outcome / Notes:** What happened? What was learned?  
7. **Next Steps:** Recommended follow-up or escalation  
8. **Related Docs:** Link to supporting protocols, guides, or annexes

---

## REFLECTION PRINCIPLES

- Use **objective, somatic, or flow-based observations** where possible  
- Note both **positive and negative effects**  
- Update **iteratively**; this table is meant to grow with experience  
- Share insights across Nodes to **accelerate systemic learning**  
- Treat **each row as a micro-case study** for continuous design improvement
