### **Month 10: Choose Economic Model**

ðŸ“– **Read:** MICRO-ECONOMY_GUIDE.md

âœ… **Do:** Decide which model fits your group

**The 3 Models:**

**Model 1: Pooled Fund** (full sharing)
- Everyone puts money in one pot
- All shared expenses paid from pot
- High trust required
- Best for: 3-5 people, long-term commitment

**Model 2: Split Bill** (proportional by income)
- Shared expenses divided by income percentage
- Feels fair (richer pay more)
- Requires income transparency
- Best for: 5-10 people, mixed incomes

**Model 3: Solidarity Network** (opt-in help)
- Everyone manages own money
- Help given when someone asks
- No repayment expected
- Best for: Loose groups, transition phase

ðŸ“ **Output:** Chosen model documented
