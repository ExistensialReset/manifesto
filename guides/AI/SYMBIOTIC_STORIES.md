## THE DAY OAKRIDGE SAID NO
**Scenario:** A surplus of energy was offered to Oakridge Node via AI.
**Decision:** Oakridge Mirrors declined. 
**Lesson:** Efficiency is not always accepting surplus. Sometimes efficiency is knowing you have enough.

## THE CHILD WHO VETOED HER OWN DATA
**Scenario:** 12-year-old Zara, a diabetic, requested that AI stop tracking her medical data for privacy.
**Decision:** Mirrors upheld her request.
**Lesson:** Agency > Efficiency. Children's veto is absolute.
