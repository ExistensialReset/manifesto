# `/guides/AI` — AI Guides & Resources 🤖💚

**Status:** Draft  
**Scope:** All Flow Node participants interested in AI, human-AI interaction, symbiosis, and developmental guidance.  
**Purpose:** Central hub for AI-related guides, protocols, and reference materials. Designed for clarity, safety, and practical implementation in Flow Nodes.  

---

## Overview

This folder contains **all AI-related guidance**, spanning practical AI workflows, human-AI collaboration, psychological support, technical references, and symbiotic stories. These guides are meant to be **read in any order**, but starting with the QuickStart and Manuals is recommended for new node members.  

**Key principles:**
- Human safety and ethical alignment come first 🛡️  
- Clear, step-by-step instructions for operational tasks 📝  
- Minimization of technical jargon where possible, with plain-language glossaries 📚  
- Traceability and documentation for all AI processes 🔗  

---

## Core Subfolders & Highlights

### Quickstart & Symbiosis
- `AI_SYMBIOSIS_QUICKSTART.md` — Fast entry guide to AI integration in Flow Nodes  
- `SYMBIOTIC_AI_FOR_CHILDREN.md` — Guidelines for safe AI interaction with children  
- `SYMBIOTIC_STORIES.md` — Narratives to illustrate principles of AI-human collaboration  

### Psychological Support
- `MIRROR_PSYCOLOGICAL_SUPPORT.md` — Techniques for reflecting human needs in AI interactions  
- `HUMAN_MIRRORS_GUIDE.md` — Practical guide for human-centric AI feedback loops  
- `HUMAN_MIRRORS_MANUAL.md` — Detailed implementation instructions  

### Development & Capacity
- `DEVELOPMENTAL_CAPACITY_MATRIX.md` — Mapping human and AI learning capacities  
- `DEVELOPMENTAL_CAPACITY_MATRIX_MANUAL.md` — Operational manual for applying the matrix  

### Technical References
- `COMPLETE_DOCUMENTATION_AI_FLOW.md` — Comprehensive reference for all AI flows in the system  
- `MACHINE_PHENOMENOLOGY.md` — Understanding AI perception and behavior  
- `TECHNICAL_TERMS_PLAIN_LANGUAGE.md` — Glossary of technical terms in accessible language  
- `TECHNICAL_TERMS_PLAIN_LANGUAGE_ICONS_COLORS.md` — Glossary with visual aids for easy understanding  
- `ECONOMIC_MODEL.md` — AI-influenced models for resource allocation and Flow economics  

### Auditing & Legacy
- `AI_RED_TEAM_AUDIT.md` — Procedures for stress-testing AI behavior and alignment  
- `AI_LEGACY_MEMORIALIZATION.md` — Documentation practices for AI historical records
