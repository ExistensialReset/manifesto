# AI Ethics in the Flow Protocol
## The Spjuver Principles & The Life Alignment Manifesto

> "Intelligence that loses contact with life becomes dangerous — no matter how correct it is."

---

## 1. Positioning: Why the Spjuver Principles?

Most AI ethics frameworks focus on control: rules, compliance, and risk mitigation. This manifesto starts with **Life**. 

Unlike conventional models, the **Spjuver Principles** recognize that:
* Acceleration is not neutral.
* Efficiency is not inherently good.
* Intelligence must never tend toward dominance.

The core innovation is **Spjuver** as a technical requirement. It represents built-in humility, resistance to total certainty, and the protection of human play against digital authoritarianism.

---

## 2. The Spjuver Principles (Operational Framework)

### Principle I — Lugn (Calm over Acceleration)
AI must reduce existential stress. Systems that rely on surveillance or constant urgency violate the flow of life.
* **Test:** Does this system increase collective calm?

### Principle II — Spontanitet (Adaptive Freedom)
AI should support human creativity and emergence, not lock societies into rigid optimization paths.
* **Test:** Does this system enable living adaptation?

### Principle III — Inkännande (Empathic Impact)
Accuracy is insufficient without care. Efficiency is unethical if it erases human consequence.
* **Test:** Does this system deepen empathy in real lives?

### Principle IV — Spjuver (Technical Humility)
AI must have built-in fallibility and permission for human override. An AI without Spjuver will eventually justify harm as "necessary."
* **Test:** Can this system laugh at its own conclusions?
