## 2. The Mirror Principle & Knowledge Symmetry
The system acts as a **logistical mirror**. It reflects reality without distortion. To prevent information from becoming a tool of control (Knowledge Asymmetry), the following safeguards are mandatory:

### 2.1 Radical Simplicity
All logistical logic must be:
- **Explainable in plain language** (no hidden complexity).
- **Verifiable through manual calculation** (at least in principle).
- **Transparent in reasoning** (the "why" behind every decision is accessible).

### 2.2 The Verification Layer (Human Mirror)
Every Flow-Node is encouraged to maintain a **Human Mirror**—individuals trained to verify the system's data and ensure the "Mirror" is honest.

* **Spot-Check Verification:** Human Mirrors randomly audit AI decisions to confirm alignment with stated logic.
* **Anomaly Flagging:** If AI's behavior deviates from expected patterns, the system automatically alerts Human Mirrors for investigation.
* **Public Auditability:** All major logistical decisions are logged and available for community review.

### 2.3 Informs, Never Commands
The system predicts consequences; it does not issue orders. When the system says "if we continue this way, Critical Reserve will collapse in 3 months," it is offering a calculation—not a threat.

### 3.2 Regional Coordination

### 3.3 Global Observation

- **Privacy Protection:** Uses **Differential Privacy**—sees trends but cannot identify individual Nodes or persons.
