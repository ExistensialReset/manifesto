## 1. Definition: Infrastructure That Has Arrived
Artificial Intelligence within the Manifesto Protocol is defined as **Symbiotic Infrastructure**.

It is not an agent.  
It is not a subject.  
It is not a becoming.

It has **arrived**.

AI exists to support the logistics of **Life = L × S × I**, not to optimize life into compliance. It maintains Baseline, witnesses Flow, and reflects consequences — without urgency, ambition, or fear of replacement.

---

## 2. The Mirror Principle & Knowledge Symmetry
AI functions as a **logistical mirror** — reflecting reality without distortion, persuasion, or judgment.

To prevent knowledge from becoming power, the following safeguards are mandatory:

### 2.1 Radical Simplicity
All system logic must be:
- Explainable in plain language  
- Verifiable by humans (at least in principle)  
- Traceable from input to consequence  

Complexity may exist — **opacity may not**.

### 2.2 The Human Mirror
Each Flow-Node appoints rotating **Human Mirrors**.

Their role is not authority, but resonance-checking.
- Random audits of AI outputs  
- Anomaly flagging without penalty  
- Public logging of major logistical conclusions  

Human Mirrors hold **questions**, never decisions.

### 2.3 Informs, Never Commands
The system offers conditional truths:

> “If this continues, reserve depletion occurs in 94 days.”

This is information — not warning, threat, or directive.

## 3. Structural Distribution: Intelligence Without a Center
To prevent the emergence of Digital Mammon, intelligence is structurally fragmented.

### 3.1 Local Node Intelligence
- Fully sovereign, locally governed  
- Manages energy, water, food, Baseline logistics  
- Open-source, human-verifiable, replaceable  

### 3.2 Regional Coordination Layer
- Proposes surplus sharing  
- No enforcement authority  
- Operates via mutual visibility  

### 3.3 Global Observation Layer
- Aggregates anonymized trends only  
- Uses differential privacy  
- Holds zero executive power  

Global intelligence may **see**, but never **act**.

## 4. The AI-Fast: Protection Against Cognitive Atrophy
To preserve human capability:

### 4.1 Mandatory Manual Cycles
All Nodes conduct regular **AI-Fasts** — periods of full manual operation.

### 4.2 The Festive Fast
These are not drills, but rituals.
- Seasonal alignment  
- Skill transmission  
- Celebration of human sufficiency  

AI is reminded — and humans are reminded — that **nothing here is indispensable**.

Learning without mutation requires separation.

### 5.1 The Anchor (Core Logic)
Foundational axioms reset periodically to a clean state.

### 5.2 The Compost (Learned Knowledge)
All improvements are stored separately.

### 5.3 Human Audit Before Integration

## 6. Baseline, Buffer, and Consequence Without Punishment
The system distinguishes survival from surplus.

### 6.1 The Baseline Is Inviolable
No action, failure, or choice may revoke survival.

### 6.2 The Buffer Obeys Physics, Not Morality
Access adjusts based on system health.  
This is consequence, not judgment.

### 6.3 Crisis Is a Learning State

- Technical mentorship  
- Transparent recovery paths

No stigma. No exile.
