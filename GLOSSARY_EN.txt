# KEYS TO THE NEW ERA: A Guide for Sovereigns

This glossary provides the necessary definitions to navigate the architecture of Existential Sovereignty.

---

### The Systemic Pillars

* *Global Capacity ($C_{global}$):* The scientific, real-time measurement of the planet's ecological health and carrying capacity. All production must adhere to this limit, guarded by the Triple-Blind Review.
* *Guaranteed Baseline (Baseline Allocation):* The irrevocable package of resources (housing, food, healthcare, and education) that belongs to every individual as a birthright. It is the elimination of the fear of survival.
* *Principle of Seven Generations:* The mandated time philosophy requiring a 50 % Safety Margin in all resource consumption to protect the well-being of future generations (approximately 200 years).

* *Post-Quantum Blockchain:* The unchangeable, decentralized digital architecture used to store the Manifest's core laws and secure data integrity.
