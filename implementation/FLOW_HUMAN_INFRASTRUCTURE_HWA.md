# FLOW_HUMAN_INFRASTRUCTURE_HWA.md
*Human Work Architecture – Rotating Task System*

---

## 1. Purpose
- Describe the **rotating work system for Flow**, integrating legit professions, semi-legit roles, and full rotation for other tasks.
- Audience: new participants, node organizers, Lyceum educators, external reviewers.

---

## 2. Principles
- **All tasks rotate** among participants except fully legit professions.
- **Baseline access** remains guaranteed.
- **Work hours** tracked for learning, contribution mapping, and fairness.
- **Safety and mentoring** prioritized.

> Operational complement to [FLOW_HUMAN_INFRASTRUCTURE.md](./FLOW_HUMAN_INFRASTRUCTURE.md)

---

## 3. Legitimate Professions
- Require certification or licensing; **work not rotated**:
  - Doctors / Nurses / Midwives
  - Psychologists / Licensed Therapists
  - Physiotherapists
  - Electricians
  - Civil Engineers
  - Lab specialists
- **Training:** via Lyceum Musaeum, mentorship, structured knowledge transfer.

---

## 4. Semi-Legit Professions
- Require experience, supervision, or skills; rotation partial under supervision:
  - Teachers / Educators
  - Junior lab technicians
  - Apprentice engineers / builders
  - Node coordinators
- Rotation maintains safety and quality.

---

## 5. Fully Rotating Tasks
- Food prep, gardening, hydroponics
- Cleaning, maintenance, logistics
- Non-specialized construction/assembly
- Peer mentoring/admin support (non-legit roles)
- **Rotation ensures fairness and prevents burnout.**

---

## 6. Lyceum Musaeum – Education Hub
- Trains legit and semi-legit participants.
- Functions:
  - Practical workshops
  - Rotating task simulation
  - Mentorship
  - Knowledge documentation and peer learning
  - Readiness evaluation
- **Integration with Flow:** progression from rotated tasks → semi-legit → legit roles.

---

## 7. Human Work Architecture (HWA) – System Overview
- Integrates baseline, rotating tasks, semi-legit training, legit professions.
- **Safety & oversight:** mentors/certified professionals supervise higher-risk tasks.
- **Documentation:** hours, outcomes, rotations logged in Flow Spiral.

---

## 8. Key Rules
1. Rotation is universal except fully legit professions.  
2. Safety first: heavy/risky tasks require mentorship.  
3. Baseline guaranteed.  
4. Progression: rotated → semi-legit → legit via Lyceum.  
5. Monitoring: hours and rotations feed Flow Spiral.  
6. Diversity: participants rotate through wide spectrum of tasks.
