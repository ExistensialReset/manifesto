## 5. Automation Levels Defined

- **Level 2:** Semi-automated irrigation and monitoring (peri-urban)  
- **Level 3:** Robotic harvest and climate control (urban)  
- **Level 4:** Full automation including planting, harvest, nutrient, and climate control (regional)

---

## 6. Integration & Flow Strategy

- Urban: mushrooms, microgreens, leafy greens (low energy)  
- Peri-Urban: nuts, legumes, berries, mushroom logs (medium energy)  
- Regional: spirulina, lentils, mung beans (high energy)  

**Flow Principles:**  
- Automation reduces labor  
- Water recirculation across nodes  
- Inter-node protein & energy trade balances high-energy crops  
- Surplus energy nodes supply deficits → full self-sufficiency
