## 7. IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

1. **Node Size Assumption:** ~500 m² hydroponic area per Node.  
2. **Multiple Harvests:** Leafy greens and herbs can be harvested continuously; pulses and fruits have discrete cycles.  
3. **Algae Production:** Requires controlled light, CO₂ supplementation, and nutrient tanks.  
4. **Integration with Baseline:** Production is primarily for Node consumption; surplus can feed regional exchanges.  
5. **Hydroponic Methods:** NFT, DWC, vertical towers, and floating rafts for algae.
