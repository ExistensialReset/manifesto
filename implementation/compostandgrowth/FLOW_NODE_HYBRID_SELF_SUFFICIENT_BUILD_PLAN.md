# FLOW NODE HYBRID – SELF-SUFFICIENT BUILD PLAN (Updated)

**Purpose:** Detailed, scalable build plan for self-sufficient Flow Node, integrating automation, energy, protein production, and inter-node coordination.

**Population:** 500 people

---

## 1. Node Types & Automation

| Node Type | Area | Automation Level | Description |
|-----------|------|-----------------|-------------|
| Urban | 500 m² | Level 3 | Indoor cultivation: mushrooms, microgreens, leafy greens. Robotic harvest, automated climate control. |
| Peri-urban | 2 ha | Level 2 | Agroforestry: nut trees, legumes, berries, mushroom logs. Semi-automated irrigation and monitoring. |
| Regional | 1 000 m² | Level 4 | High-energy crops: spirulina, lentils, mung beans. Fully automated planting, harvest, nutrient delivery, and climate regulation. |

**Automation Levels:**
- Level 2: Semi-automated irrigation & monitoring.
- Level 3: Robotic harvest, automated climate control, LED scheduling.
- Level 4: Full automation including planting, harvesting, nutrient control, climate management.

## 5. Inter-Node Flow Strategy

- **Urban:** Low-energy protein (mushrooms, microgreens, leafy greens)
- **Peri-urban:** Medium-energy protein (nuts, legumes, berries, mushroom logs)
- **Regional:** High-energy protein (spirulina, lentils, mung beans)

**Principles:**
1. Urban nodes maximize högvärdig snabbväxande proteinproduktion.
2. Peri-urban nodes använder mark och agroforestry för stabil produktion.
3. Regional nodes producerar energikrävande grödor där energi finns.
4. Surplus energy or protein can flow between nodes according to demand.

---

## 6. Recommendations & Next Steps

- Prioritize mushrooms and legumes in low-energy nodes.
- Spirulina and other high-energy crops in energy-surplus nodes.
- Maximize automation and LED efficiency.
- Implement inter-node protein & energy trade protocols.
- Continuous monitoring of production, energy, and water usage.
- Run three parallel pilot nodes (urban, peri-urban, regional), then optimize.
