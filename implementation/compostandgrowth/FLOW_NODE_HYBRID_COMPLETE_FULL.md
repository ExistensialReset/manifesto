## 8. Inter-Node Flow Strategy

1. Urban nodes focus on high-value, low-energy protein.  
2. Peri-urban nodes manage medium-energy protein crops using land/ecosystem services.  
3. Regional nodes produce high-energy protein where energy is abundant.  
4. Surplus protein & energy flows to deficit nodes.  
5. Storage only covers short-term imbalances; seasonal coordination is necessary.

---
