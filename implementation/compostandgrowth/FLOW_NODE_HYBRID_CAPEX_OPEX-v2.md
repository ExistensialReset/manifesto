# FLOW NODE HYBRID – CAPEX & OPEX ANALYSIS

**Purpose:** Realistic financial and resource planning for a 500-person Flow Hybrid Node, including land, buildings, automation, energy storage, and operational costs.

**Assumptions:**
- Urban Node: 500 m², Automation Level 3  
- Peri-Urban Node: 2 ha, Automation Level 2  
- Regional Node: 1 000 m², Automation Level 4  
- All costs in USD, based on 2026 regional averages  
- Lifespan: Buildings 30 years, hydroponics 10–15 years, LED 5–7 years, robotics 10 years, batteries 10–15 years

## 4. Notes & Assumptions

   - Level 4: Full automation incl. planting, harvesting, nutrients, climate (Regional)
2. Lifespan:
   - Equipment replaced according to lifespan; depreciation considered over lifespan.
3. Energy Storage:
   - Regional node includes 100 kWh battery modules for storing surplus energy.
4. Land & Building:
   - Costs include standard construction and acquisition costs based on 2026 averages.
5. All values are indicative; local market variations must be applied.
