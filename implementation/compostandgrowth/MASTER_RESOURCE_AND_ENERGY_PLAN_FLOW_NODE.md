## 9. INTER-NODE FLOW STRATEGY

1. Urban nodes: high-value, low-energy protein  
2. Peri-Urban nodes: medium-energy protein using land/ecosystem services  
3. Regional nodes: high-energy protein where energy is abundant  
4. Surplus protein & energy flows to deficit nodes  
5. Short-term storage only; seasonal coordination required

- Distributed resilience via inter-node surplus flow  
- Avoid ecological disruption (no mega-dams)  
- Adaptive scaling for new renewable technologies
