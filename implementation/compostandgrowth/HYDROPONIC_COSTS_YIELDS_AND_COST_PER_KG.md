## 1. PURPOSE

Provides **setup costs, annual operating costs, expected hydroponic yields, total production, and cost per kg of yield** for Flow Nodes worldwide.  

---

## 2. SCOPE

- Hydroponic Nodes in Flow system  
- Regions: Europe, North America, South America, Oceania, Asia, North Africa, Sub-Saharan Africa  
- Costs in USD, yields in kg, cost per kg in USD/kg

---

## 3. ASSUMPTIONS

- Node size: 500 m² per Node  
- Crops: leafy greens, herbs, legumes (lentils), algae  
- Infrastructure: hydroponic system, climate control, pumps, sensors  
- Energy: mix of solar and grid  
- Labor: regional averages  
- Local logistics included  
- Node counts estimated for deployment
