## 1. PURPOSE

This document provides **realistic per-kilogram hydroponic production costs** for multiple crops in Nodes worldwide. Costs include **amortized setup** and **annual operating expenses**, adjusted per region.  

---

## 2. ASSUMPTIONS

- Node area: 500 m² vertical hydroponic system  
- System: NFT/DWC + LED lighting  
- Yield estimates per 500 m² Node/year:  
  - Leafy greens: 20,000 kg  
  - Herbs (basil, mint, parsley): 5,000 kg  
  - Microalgae: 3,000 kg  
  - Hydroponic lentils: 4,000 kg  
  - Tomatoes: 7,000 kg  
- Setup amortized over 5 years  
- Costs in **USD**, adjusted for labour & energy per region  

---

## 3. PER-KG COST FORMULA
Per-kg cost = (Setup Cost / 5 years + Annual Operating Cost) / Annual Yield
