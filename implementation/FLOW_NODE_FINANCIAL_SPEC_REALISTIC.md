## 9. Sensitivity Analysis

| Factor                     | Adjusted Value       | Effect on CAPEX / OPEX |
|-----------------------------|-------------------|-----------------------|
| Peri-urban Land Price       | 50,000 USD/ha      | +80,000 USD CAPEX |
| Peri-urban Land Price       | 100,000 USD/ha     | +180,000 USD CAPEX |
| Wind Turbine Cost           | 8,000 USD/turbine  | +360,000 USD CAPEX (Regional) |
| Battery Storage Duration    | Extended 2 days    | +50,000 USD CAPEX |
| Labor Costs (Region Variance)| ±20%               | OPEX adjusts proportionally |
| Building Cost Variance      | ±15%                | CAPEX adjusts proportionally |

**Notes:**

- Storage increase for longer fluctuations (cloudy/windless periods) may require hydrogen or pumped hydro  
- Regional CAPEX is most sensitive to wind turbine cost and high-tech building materials  
- Labor costs vary by country (cheaper in Asia/Africa, higher in Europe/North America)  
- Energy production assumptions assume solar panel efficiency of 18% and average daily solar insolation (kWh/m²)
