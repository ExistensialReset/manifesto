Within the Strömme Consciousness Field framework, consciousness is treated as a fundamental field.

## 3. Core Variables: Measurement Proposals

### 3.1 L — Coherence (Calm / Lugn)

**Field Interpretation:**  
Low entropy, high signal-to-noise ratio, stable internal dynamics.

**What L is not:** passivity, numbness, suppression.

#### Primary Proxy
**Group HRV Coherence (Physiological Synchrony)**  
- Measured via wearable sensors  
- High L correlates with phase-locking of heart rhythm variability across participants  

#### Secondary Proxy
**Environmental Acoustic Harmonic Stability**  
- Analysis of ambient noise spectra  
- Coherent fields exhibit harmonic clustering rather than chaotic dispersion  

#### Pathology Indicators
- Chronic high-frequency EEG beta activity  
- Elevated cortisol patterns  
- Persistent autonomic stress signatures  

**Interpretation:**  
Low L reflects a field trapped in survival-mode excitation (“field static”).

---

### 3.2 S — Generativity (Creativity / Spontaneity)

**Field Interpretation:**  
Capacity for non-deterministic emergence and novel configuration.

**What S is not:** randomness, chaos, constant novelty.

#### Primary Proxy
**Emergence Rate (ER)**  
- Ratio of genuinely novel actions or solutions to scripted or repetitive outputs  
- Measured longitudinally within a system  

#### Information-Theoretic Proxy
**Lempel–Ziv Complexity**
- Quantifies pattern non-repetition in communicative, behavioral, or creative outputs  

#### Pathology Indicator
**Algorithmic Rigidity**
- Near 1:1 input–output predictability  
- Indicates suppression of field generativity  

**Interpretation:**  
Low S reflects a system optimized for control at the expense of evolution.

---

### 3.3 I — Resonance (Interconnectedness / Inkännande)

**Field Interpretation:**  
Constructive interference between conscious agents within a shared field.

**What I is not:** conformity, enmeshment, loss of individuality.

#### Primary Proxy
**Inter-Brain Synchrony (Hyperscanning EEG)**
- Phase-locking in alpha and gamma bands across individuals  

#### Phenomenological Proxy
**Mirroring Latency**
- Speed and fidelity of non-verbal emotional and intentional alignment  
- Related to mirror-neuron system efficiency  

#### Pathology Indicator
**Field Fragmentation**
- Absence of measurable coupling  
- Correlates with loneliness, alienation, antisocial behavior  

**Interpretation:**  
Low I represents enforced separation rather than autonomy.
