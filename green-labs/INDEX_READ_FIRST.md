## READ THIS FIRST

This directory contains **materials that exist because access to medicine is not guaranteed**.

The presence of medical recipes or formulations in this archive is **not an endorsement**.
It is evidence.

Evidence that people, when denied affordable and timely care, begin to document survival knowledge that should never have been their responsibility to carry.

---

## WHY THIS EXISTS

In large parts of the world, people cannot reliably access the medications they depend on.

- In the United States, there is **no national high-cost protection**.
- In other regions, shortages, patents, pricing, or gatekeeping create similar gaps.
- Even in countries with partial protection, cost ceilings can still exceed what many can pay.

When medicine becomes unaffordable, **people do not stop needing it**.
They look for other ways.

This directory exists to document that pressure.

## ABOUT THE CONTENT
