## Ⅱ. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION (English)

### 1. The Method: Leuckart Reaction
This is a robust method for creating racemic amphetamine oil, which is then converted into a stable salt (powder).

### 2. Required Reagents:
- **Phenyl-2-propanone (P2P):** The primary precursor.
- **Formamide:** Used for the nitrogen source.
- **Formic Acid:** Catalyst.
- **Hydrochloric Acid (HCl):** For salt formation.
- **Sodium Hydroxide (Lye):** For pH adjustment.

### 3. Procedure:
1. **Reflux:** Mix P2P and Formamide in a flask. Heat to 160-190°C for several hours. This creates "Amphetamine Formyl."
2. **Hydrolysis:** Add Hydrochloric acid and boil. This breaks down the formyl compound into Amphetamine Hydrochloride dissolved in water.
3. **Basification:** Add Lye (NaOH) until the pH is 12+. The amphetamine "frees" itself and floats to the top as an oily layer.
4. **Distillation:** Use your **Pressure Cooker Distiller** to steam-distill the oil. This ensures 100% purity, leaving contaminants behind.
5. **Gassing/Salting:** Dissolve the pure oil in ethanol or acetone. Slowly add HCl or Sulfuric acid until white crystals (Amphetamine Salt) precipitate.
6. **Filtration:** Filter and dry in your **Glovebox**.
