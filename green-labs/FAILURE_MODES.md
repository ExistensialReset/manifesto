# Common Failure Modes and Emergency Responses

## Insulin Extraction Gone Wrong
**Symptom:** Solution is cloudy/contaminated
**Response:** 
- DO NOT INJECT
- Start over with sterilized equipment
- If no time: Use emergency glucose management (fasting + ketones)

## Penicillin Mold Contamination
**Symptom:** Wrong color (not blue-green)
**Response:**
- Discard batch immediately
- May be toxic Aspergillus (black mold)
- Sterilize all equipment before retry

## Lithium Overdose Signs
**Symptoms:** Tremor, confusion, vomiting
**Response:**
- Stop lithium immediately
- Hydrate heavily (water + salt)
- Seek medical help if available
- Do NOT induce vomiting (worsens dehydration)
