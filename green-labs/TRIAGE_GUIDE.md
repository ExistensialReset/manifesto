# TRIAGE GUIDE: What to Make First When Crisis Hits

## Tier 1: Life-or-Death (Within 24-48 hours)
1. **Insulin** (if diabetic)
2. **Naloxone** (if opioid users in community)
3. **Activated Charcoal** (universal antidote)

## Tier 2: Acute Stabilization (Within 1 week)
1. **Lithium Carbonate** (bipolar crisis)
2. **Thyroid NDT** (metabolic collapse)
3. **Penicillin** (infection without antibiotics)

## Tier 3: Ongoing Stability (Within 1 month)
1. **Buspirone/Inderal** (anxiety/stress management)
2. **Cannabis oil** (pain/sleep)
3. **Esomeprazole** (gastric protection)

## Tier 4: Quality of Life (As resources allow)
1. **Hashish** (controlled symptom relief)
2. **Duloxetine** (depression/nerve pain)
