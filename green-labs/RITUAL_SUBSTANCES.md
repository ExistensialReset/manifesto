## Ⅱ. PSILOCYBIN EXTRACTION (From Fungi)
**Purpose:** Deep empathy, neuroplasticity, and ego-dissolution.  
**Source:** *Psilocybe cubensis* (Cultivated in your Glovebox/SAB).

### 1. Requirements:
- Dried fungi (cracker dry).
- 95% Food-grade Ethanol.
- Glass jars and your **Magnetic Stirrer**.

### 2. Process:
1. **Grinding:** Pulverize the dried fungi into a fine dust. This increases the surface area for the solvent.
2. **Maceration:** Submerge the powder in ethanol. Place on the **Magnetic Stirrer** for 24 hours. The constant motion ensures the ethanol pulls every molecule of psilocybin out of the chitin.
3. **Filtration:** Filter through a fine mesh, then a coffee filter, until the liquid is clear and blue-tinted.
4. **Evaporation:** Place the liquid in a shallow dish inside your **Glovebox**. Let the ethanol evaporate naturally.
5. **Result:** You are left with a concentrated resin. 
**Safety Note:** Extraction allows for precise milligram dosing, bypassing the unpredictable potency of raw mushrooms and reducing stomach distress.

## Ⅲ. DMT EXTRACTION (The "Spirit Molecule")
**Purpose:** Short, intense visionary states; "The Systemic Reset."  
**Source:** *Mimosa hostilis* root bark (MHRB).

### 1. Requirements:
- Shredded MHRB.
- Sodium Hydroxide (Lye from your soap making).
- Vinegar (Acetic acid).
- Heptane or Naptha (Non-polar solvent).

### 2. Process:
1. **Acid Phase:** Simmer the bark in water and vinegar (pH 3). This converts DMT into a water-soluble salt.
2. **Base Phase:** Slowly add Lye until pH is 12+. The solution turns black. The DMT is now a "freebase" molecule.
3. **The Pull:** Add heptane. Use the **Magnetic Stirrer** on the lowest setting. The DMT will migrate into the heptane.
4. **Freeze-Precipitation:** Syringe out the heptane and place it in a glass jar in the freezer for 24 hours. 
5. **Harvest:** Pure DMT crystals will form on the glass. Pour off the heptane and dry the crystals in your **Glovebox**.
