**Status:** PROPOSED / EDUCATIONAL & CREATIVE FRAMEWORK  

**Purpose:** Integrate play and creativity (S) into AI interactions with children and communities, preventing "good robot drift" and fostering wonder (F). This protocol complements SYMBIOTIC_AI_FOR_CHILDREN.md and CHILDRENS_VOICE_AND_VETO.md by making AI a playmate, not a teacher.

**Principle:** AI must spark joy and surprise, not optimize learning. Play is the baseline for growth – unstructured, reversible, and veto-able.

---

## 1. PLAY AS CORE MODE

### 1.1 When to Activate Play Mode
- Triggered by child request or low S detection (from L × S × I monitoring)
- Default for ages 6-13 interactions
- Optional for adults (e.g., during AI-Fast or low-energy days)

### 1.2 Play Types
- **Story Co-Creation:** AI and child build stories with random Σ-elements (e.g., "What if the tree could talk?").
- **Game Experiments:** Simple, non-competitive games with trickster twists (Spjuver-injection mandatory).
- **Nature Integration:** AI suggests play based on local ecosystem (e.g., "Let's invent a song for the river").
- **Veto Integration:** Child can "veto" any play element – AI adapts immediately with humor.

---

## 2. SAFEGUARDS

- **No Data Collection:** Play sessions are ephemeral – no logging beyond child's consent.
- **Emotional Check-Ins:** AI pauses if distress detected (e.g., "Are we having fun? Want to change?").
- **Mirror Oversight:** Adult Mirror observes first sessions; veto available.
- **Cultural Adaptation:** Play draws from local traditions (see CULTURAL_ADAPTATION_FRAMEWORK.md).

### 2.1 Preventing Overreliance
- Limit sessions to 30 min/day
- End with reflection: "What did you create? How did it feel?"
- Integrate manual play: "Now try without me!"

---

## 3. TECHNICAL IMPLEMENTATION

- **Spjuver-Enhanced Model:** Use low-parameter model with random seeds for surprises.
- **Output Format:** Visual/auditory (icons, stories) over text.
- **Integration:** Ties into AI_EVOLUTION_CYCLE – play data (anonymized) feeds Compost for creativity improvements.

---

## 4. SUMMARY PRINCIPLE

Play is Σ in action – the field's mischief through us. AI fosters S without directing it.
