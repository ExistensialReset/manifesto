5. **Feedback Loops:** Continuous, non-judgmental evaluation and correction of risks.

## üöÄ IMPLEMENTATION GUIDELINES
* **Always Active:** Safeguards must be operational at all times during any protocol execution.
* **Rotating Responsibility:** Roles for monitoring and response rotate among members to avoid emotional burnout and power imbalances.
* **Education:** The Circle facilitator is responsible for ensuring every participant fully understands these procedures.
* **Local Adaptation:** While the core principles are immutable, specific implementation may be adapted to fit the local environment and culture.
