## 1. Core Node Roles

| Role | Scope | Responsibilities |
|------|-------|----------------|
| Node Lead | Node | Oversee daily operations, ensure energy & communication protocols followed, coordinate local LOTUS panel |
| Info Steward | Node | Maintain information flow, prune channels, report accessibility issues |
| Technical Guardian | Node | Maintain hardware sovereignty, mesh network integrity, energy monitoring |
| Regional Coordinator | Regional | Aggregate Node reports, coordinate cross-Node decisions, escalate critical risks to Global LOTUS |
| LOTUS Panelist | Global | Participate in lottery-selected decision-making, enforce baseline knowledge & Flow principles |

---

## 2. Governance via LOTUS

- All **Regional & Global Nodes** operate under LOTUS Protocol.  
- Panel formation, mandates, rotations, and audits follow **LOTUS rules**.  
- Regional Node Coordinators are selected through **LOTUS lottery** to maintain fairness and integrity.

---

## 3. Accountability & Documentation

1. Each role **maintains logs** of actions, decisions, and energy reports.  
2. Decisions requiring cross-Node coordination are **documented and mirrored**.  
3. LOTUS panels validate that Node operations follow **baseline knowledge, non-coercion, and post-monetary principles**.  
4. Role rotation schedules are enforced to prevent concentration of power.
