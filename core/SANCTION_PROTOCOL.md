# SANCTION_PROTOCOL.md

**Status:** ACTIVE  
**Layer:** Governance Integrity  
**Scope:** Node Misconduct or Manipulation  
**Selection:** All decision panels selected via LOTUS  

---

# 1. CORE PRINCIPLES

- Protect Baseline first  
- Apply lowest sufficient intervention  
- Ensure proportionality  
- No collective punishment  
- All actions documented  

Sanctions apply to governance structure — not individuals' basic rights.

---

# 2. SANCTION ESCALATION MODEL

## Level 1 — Process Correction

Conditions:
- First-time discrepancy
- No Baseline threat

Actions:
- Increased audit frequency
- Mandatory external verification
- Public discrepancy report

Duration: Time-limited.

---

## Level 2 — Autonomy Restriction

Conditions:
- Repeated manipulation
- Intentional misreporting

Actions:
- Regional co-signature required for critical transfers
- Expanded audit scope
- Temporary loss of expansion rights

---

## Level 3 — Infrastructure Co-Management

Conditions:
- Baseline risk identified
- Systemic governance failure

Actions:
- Federative temporary infrastructure oversight
- Preservation of local cultural autonomy
- Restoration benchmarks required

---

## Level 4 — Node Suspension

Conditions:
- Active sabotage
- Refusal of cooperation
- Severe Baseline endangerment

Actions:
- Suspension of Node status
- Direct federative protection of individuals
- Governance restructuring

Extreme measure only.

---

# 3. DECISION MODEL

- Panel selected via LOTUS  
- Simple majority vote  
- Written reasoning required  
- Time-limited mandate  

---

All sanctions automatically trigger post-hoc review.
