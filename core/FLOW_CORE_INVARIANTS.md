# FLOW_CORE_INVARIANTS.md

**Status:** Core Principle  
**Scope:** Entire Flow System  
**Purpose:** Define the immutable foundations of Flow — the principles that **never change**, regardless of protocols, processes, or experiments.

---

## 1. Post-Monetary Existence
- No money, no debts in any monetary sense.  
- Exchange is based on contribution, knowledge, and mutual support, never on currency.

## 2. Baseline as Human Default
- Baseline is inherent and universal — simply by existing, one has a Baseline.  
- Includes natural curiosity, chemical/experimental exploration, and the right to grow.

## 3. Non-Coercion
- Participation is voluntary; no one can be forced.  
- All actions respect autonomy and consent.

## 4. Personal Data Rights
- All information about individuals is **directly anonymized** in storage.  
- Each individual may erase their personal data **once per year**; only essential medical records are retained in encrypted form.

## 5. LxSxI: Calm × Spontaneity × Empathy
- Life and interactions are guided by this principle.  
- Ensures **wellbeing, creativity, and mindful collaboration** across all Flow operations.

---

## 6. Compass for Decision-Making
- Every protocol, panel, or Flow experiment **must honor these invariants**.  
- They are the **true north** for all governance, lotteries, and operational rules.
