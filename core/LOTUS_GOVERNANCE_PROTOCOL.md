# LOTUS_GOVERNANCE_PROTOCOL.md
## Version 1.0
## Status: ACTIVE
## Scope: Global
## Amendment Requirement: 66% Global LOTUS Majority

---

# PURPOSE

LOTUS is the governance mechanism of Flow.

It replaces:

• Permanent political classes
• Wealth-weighted voting
• Ideological party capture

Governance is executed through randomized, rotating citizen juries.

Power is temporary.
Authority is conditional.
Rotation is mandatory.

---

# I. CORE STRUCTURE

LOTUS operates in three layers:

1. Node LOTUS (local)
2. Regional LOTUS
3. Global LOTUS

Each layer:

• Is randomly selected
• Is time-limited
• Cannot self-renew

---

# II. SELECTION MECHANISM

Participants are selected via verifiable randomization from:

• All active Nodes above Baseline
• Individuals not currently under sanction
• Individuals not serving in current LOTUS rotation

Eligibility requires:

• Baseline compliance
• No active governance privilege
• Minimum participation history threshold

Selection algorithm must be open-source and publicly auditable.

---

# III. TERM LENGTH

Node LOTUS: 30 days  
Regional LOTUS: 60 days  
Global LOTUS: 90 days  

No immediate re-selection allowed for 1 year.

Prevents power recycling.

---

# IV. DECISION THRESHOLDS

Standard Decisions:

• Simple majority (≥ 51%)

Structural Amendments:

• 66% supermajority

Emergency Override:

• 75% supermajority
• Automatic expiration within 30 days

**Administrative support cannot guide decisions**

---

# V. TRANSPARENCY REQUIREMENT

All LOTUS decisions:

• Publicly logged
• Time-stamped
• Fully documented

Individual votes may be:

• Anonymous publicly
• Cryptographically verified

Governance must be auditable.


**All briefing materials must be publicly accessible.**

---

# VI. CONFLICT OF INTEREST RULE

If a LOTUS member:

• Has direct surplus stake in decision
• Has relational stake in decision
• Is personally affected beyond general population

They must recuse automatically.

Violation triggers review.

---

# VII. POWER LIMITATION PRINCIPLE

LOTUS may:

• Amend protocols
• Trigger review mechanisms
• Override in emergencies
• Initiate audits

LOTUS may not:

• Redefine Baseline unilaterally
• Suspend Exit rights
• Eliminate fork possibility
• Permanently centralize authority

---

# VIII. COMPENSATION

LOTUS service is considered:

• Civic duty
• Temporarily protected time
• Baseline-secured period

Service cannot result in economic punishment.

---

# IX. STRUCTURAL AXIOM

Governance must circulate
just as resources circulate.

Power that does not rotate
becomes extraction.

# X. DELIBERATION REQUIREMENT

Before any vote is finalized, LOTUS must conduct a documented deliberation phase.

Deliberation includes:

• Presentation of the issue
• Submission of supporting and opposing arguments
• Clarification questions
• Written summary of the discussion

Voting without documented deliberation is invalid.

The purpose of LOTUS is not only decision-making,
but collective reasoning.
