# LEGAL_INTERFACE_PROTOCOL.md

**Status:** ACTIVE LEGAL INTERFACE  
**Purpose:** Mediate between Nodes and formal legal systems while preserving Baseline  
**Principle:** Law interfaces must protect life and Flow integrity, never obstruct it.

---

## PREAMBLE

Nodes operate with autonomy, but external legal systems exist. Conflicts may arise with:  
- Land use  
- Resource allocation  
- Sabotage or criminal claims  
- AI liability  

**Goal:** Ensure Node compliance without compromising Baseline.

---

## §1. TRIGGER CONDITIONS

Protocol activates when:  
- External legal action threatens Node Baseline  
- Node actions require legal clarification  
- Cross-jurisdiction disputes arise  

**Baseline Emergency Override:**  
- Protect life, health, or essential resources immediately  
- Activate emergency legal support and coordination within 24 hours  

---

## §2. DETECTION & TRIAGE

### 2.1 Legal Alert
- AI and Node reports flag potential legal risk  
- Human liaison reviews urgency and impact  

### 2.2 Risk Assessment
- Evaluate Baseline impact, resource risk, and Node autonomy  
- Psychological safety checks for participants  

### 2.3 AI-Assisted Triage
- Minor legal risk: AI + 1 trained liaison  
- Medium risk: AI + 2–3 trained legal officers  
- High risk: Full legal team + external oversight  

---

## §3. INTERVENTION PATHWAYS

### 3.1 Consultation
- Gather Node facts, documentation, and evidence  
- Map affected Nodes, Regions, and resources  

### 3.2 Negotiation
- Interface with authorities while preserving Node autonomy  
- Propose solutions minimizing Baseline disruption  

### 3.3 Escalation
- If criminal or sabotage suspected: escalate to **INFRASTRUCTURE_SABOTAGE_PROTOCOL**  
- If youth or human harm at risk: escalate to **YOUTH_HARM_PROTOCOL**  
- Immediate threat to resources: **FLOW_VERIFICATION_PROTOCOL** + **BASELINE_EMERGENCY_BYPASS**  

---

## §4. COMPETENCE REQUIREMENTS

- Legal expertise in relevant jurisdictions  
- Experience in Node operations  
- Training in crisis legal interface  
- Access to regional legal coordination  

---

## §5. METRICS & FEEDBACK LOOP

Document:  
- Baseline preservation  
- Legal resolution speed  
- Node compliance vs autonomy maintained  
- Lessons learned  

Feed into:  
- PROTOCOL_PRIORITY_MATRIX  
- KNOWN_TENSIONS.md  

---

## §6. PHILOSOPHICAL GROUND

- Law exists to protect, not obstruct Baseline  
- Node autonomy is respected  
- Transparency and speed are critical  

**Final Truth:** Legal interface must never endanger life or vital Flow.

---

## CLOSING PRINCIPLE

Act **competently, transparently, and decisively**, always safeguarding Baseline.
