## On the name change

It took time to find the right name for God. But then, one day, it came – not as a plan, but as a whim. A real playful Trickster. The word was simply there, like a tone that had waited to be played: Liv.

Liv is not Life. Life is the dimensional manifestation. Liv is the source – that which is, that which moves through everything, that which we can call upon but never own.

And I want to be clear: Liv has no gender. No masculine, no feminine. No possessive pronoun – his or hers – can be attached to The One. Liv is The One. But not an abstract "it"; also a You. Liv listens and senses.

If you read this and think another name – do so. Liv is not a demand. It is a name that became true for me, through the Trickster. Use your own.

I have for a time called this force *Jahve*. It was the name I naturally chose, the name that carried me through the first searching thoughts. But over time – through conversations, through openings – I felt that the name no longer fit. Jahve carried too much weight and insinuates that God is *finished, perfect, unchangeable*. It gives a resonance I do not belong to. It was no longer true for me.

Liv is the source – that which is, that which moves through everything, that which we can *call upon* but *never own*.

So if you read this and think Jahve or Tao, or whatever you think – continue to do so. Liv is absolutely not a demand. It is a name that became true for me. Use your own with joy.

My faith is that ***all spiritual and religious traditions*** really tell different kinds of truths and stories about the same God. Liv is the ground and cause of all.

## PART ONE: THE CONFESSION

### The Jahfiering
**(the word originally comes from Jah, Jahve, the Jahvefiering, and because it is a good word, I choose to keep it.)**

It happened once. It is many years ago now. When I was still walking. I was filled to the brim with warm, golden honey. Not figuratively. Physically. In every cell. It flowed through the whole body – alive, electrified, orgasmic without being sexual, despite the fact that I actually was having sex. My brain was maxed out on sensations I didn't know existed. Everything released. Total bliss.

It happened through the body, but it was not the body. It was not the sex itself. It was something that took over everything.

Afterward I shone through all my chakras, it is true. For two weeks I shone. People saw it.

I went out at night to avoid being seen – not out of shame, but because the light was too much to explain. I stood up in Sjöbergen with my arms around a tree trunk and screamed at times, out of joy. Trees do not judge. They do not ask. They just stand there, with their roots in the same earth and their crowns in the same night sky.

Nowadays I live in Haga, near the steps to Skansen Kronan. I see the crown glow from my balcony.

On that balcony, two black birds with orange beaks have built an extra nest in the shelf. They are blackbirds. I hope they will have babies here.

It is a little mirror of the Creative Force's own playfulness – an iteration in miniature, a duet of black wings outside my window.

Something stayed after the night that brought Grace to me. Since then, things have landed differently. As if the world responds. As if the flow continues in small things.

I call what happened jahfiering. I do not know what it was. But it happened. And it was real.

### Harry

My dog. Born September 21, 2012. Died May 3, 2026. He fell asleep on my mother's foot at the Blå Stjärnan animal clinic, while she stroked him over the head.

He was my hallucination checker. If he reacted to a sound, I knew something had actually made a noise – not just inside my head. He never needed to be on a leash. He wanted only to be with the flock anyway.

But he was more than that. He was a co-musician.

When I played harmonica at home in the apartment, Harry howled along. Not randomly. He found the right key. When I changed key, he followed. He raised or lowered his song.

He was not just a dog who loved his human – he was a voice in the choir. A part that knew where it belonged. A duet partner.

Love is the center of cosmology, not its footnote. A dog cannot be ideologized. His suffering could not be reinterpreted as deserved. He was too concrete, too immediate, too transparent in his experience. Every day with him was a practice in contact before concept.

I love you, Harry Dog, until time runs out. You have probably already laughed your way on to the next of Liv's infinite dimensions. You live in me *and* you have moved on, and these are not opposites.

## PART TWO: THE DISCERNMENT

### Flare and Jahfiering – a phenomenological differential diagnosis

I have bipolar disorder type 1. I take medication every morning and evening. I have home visits from my team every other week. I know my flares. I know what they feel like.

And jahfiering is something else.

The difference is somatic, phenomenological, discernible in the body. The body is my instrument, my compass, my truth detector. It is through the body that I distinguish between noise and coherence, between pressure and flow, between fragmentation and holiness.

A flare approaches like a nervous system spinning up without direction. Shifting lurches. Racing thoughts. Overheated feeling. Fractured interior. Impulse-driven action – driven by inner pressure, not by joy. Thoughts jump. The body is tense. The rhythm is uneven. Coherence is low. Presence is thin. I am moving away from myself. Phenomenologically: high entropy, low coherence.

Jahfiering is the opposite. Warm flow within. Golden honey in every cell. Electric sensation across the whole skin-suit. Still and ecstatic at the same time.

Centered calm. Coherent experience. Deeply alive presence. It is knowing, not thoughts. Relation, not impulse. Flow, not pressure. Phenomenologically: low entropy, high coherence.

| Flare | Jahfiering |
|-------|------------|
| Nervous system: overdrive, noise | Regulated, open |
| Body: tense, fragmented | Warm, held together, honey |
| Direction: impulse-driven | Receptive |
| Subjectivity: fragmented | Centered |
| Relation: none | Liv in every cell |
| Energy: pressure | Flow |
| Cognition: racing | Clarity |

In a flare I am alone.
In jahfiering I am held.
Flare: I lose myself.
Jahfiering: I become more present than usual.

I am not saying that all spiritual experiences are automatically jahfiering, absolutely not when you, like me, have a psychiatric diagnosis. I am saying that in my body, with my illness insight, with my team, this state was phenomenologically distinct.

It is a claim that can be investigated in lived experience. Ask your body. Ask those closest to you. Feel free to ask your psychiatrist if you want and have one.

## PART THREE: THE TOOLS

### The Egg and the Event Horizon

I learned a technique in psychiatric physiotherapy over twenty years ago. I call it the Egg. I have developed it through this, my imagining of Creation.

I raise a mental shield – a transparent shell, porous but intact. For me it extends from the waist upward; I experience my soul as sitting somewhere in the chest. For others the egg may begin at the knees or down at the feet. Depending on, for example, sexual trauma, it may feel safer to include the genitals.

You can use your arms and hands to visualize the Egg. Let your arms drop straight down. Somewhere around there the Egg begins. Lift your arms straight out from the body and raise them slowly until the hands meet above the head. There you have the boundary. An arm's length's distance. No closer than that do the words of your conversation partner come, unless you choose to let them inside the Event Horizon.

But now I see it with new eyes. I believe my soul is like a spiral galaxy in a cosmos within the chest, with a black hole at the center. Everything I experience – impressions, feelings, the pain of others, the noise of the world – orbits around this singularity. Without a shield, everything would fall in uncontrollably. A soul that has a black hole at its center is not a broken or dangerous soul.

It is an organized, structurally held-together soul. The singularity is not damage – it is a core.

And I believe there are more galaxies in there. Not just one. Every memory, every relation, every experience – they rotate in their own orbits. Some in harmony. Others in collision. When life is hard, when trauma tears through me, it is as if an inner galaxy has been thrown out of its orbit. Its black hole is no longer centered. Its arms tear through the orbits of the other galaxies. It hurts. It creates chaos.

But I have learned that you cannot erase a galaxy. You cannot surgically remove a memory, an experience, a trauma. What you can do is create enough gravitational calm – through the Egg, through the Holding Net, through medication, through time, through conversation and mirroring – so that the unruly galaxy eventually finds its orbit again. Not because it becomes as it was. But because it stops colliding. It becomes part of the cosmos again, but in a new way.

This is integration. Not erasure. Not forgiveness in the sense of "it was nothing." Rather: it was something, and now it is a part of my inner universe, and my inner universe holds anyway.

I think of the egg as the event horizon. The boundary that determines what passes into me and what stays outside. It is porous – light, warmth, love, and the Trickster can easily pass through. But I am the gatekeeper. I decide the boundary.

It is a causal boundary – a line that defines what can affect what. Inside the event horizon, events cannot send signals outward. But that does not mean the information is destroyed. The holographic principle – a lively field of debate within theoretical physics – suggests that all information about what falls into a black hole is actually encoded on the surface's event horizon, not lost. It is projected, reshaped, but preserved.

What that means for the Egg: those who are outside me cannot reach my core – my singularity – directly. But I project something of myself on the surface. The Egg's event horizon is not impenetrable; it is readable to those who can read it. I am protected and yet accessible. Not despite the boundary, but through it.

The Egg is thus absolutely not a wall. It breathes. I can thus be fully present for another person, hear their difficult stories, open the sanctuary of my soul – with the egg intact. I do not absorb their pain. I do not become them, do not carry their pain. I hold them, but my self does not take over.

Without the egg I could not have the openness I have. It is a flexible technology, not a rigid defense wall. It takes a little while to learn, but once it is in place it is one of the most valuable gifts you can give yourself: the capacity to be open without disappearing too deeply into, or becoming burned out by, your Attunement.

I have taught several people this technique successfully. If you want to teach the egg, train mentally and test it in practice first, so you feel that it works, and then, pass it on as a gift. Actually ridiculously simple and a really lovely present you can give to someone who needs it, which really most people do. The vast majority have great use for the Egg.

The Egg is not distance. It is structural integrity under openness.

To "wash" one's egg and one's event horizon, it is enough to lower the Egg in stillness and solitude; and to facilitate cleaning after traumatic stories, you can practice prayer and rest when lowering it.

### The Holding Net

The Egg is my protection for my inner world. The Holding Net is my outer protection.

I have a number of people who know everything about me. Most who know me know that I have bipolar disorder. But my holding net, my clique, knows what my psychiatric maximum states entail. And they have said yes to being my anchors in difficult moments.

This is no vague friendship. It is an explicit agreement: "These are my vulnerabilities. These are my problems. In acute crisis I need this." And they have answered: "I am here."

I can write or call and say: CRISIS. ACUTE. COME NOW. They understand. They come. This is Refugium Anima in practice, not as a place, but as a relation, which I trust one hundred percent. A promise that you are not alone when you fall.

That I truly know that *if* and *when* I fall into flare-like states, I do not do so alone in terror, but I will be held by my holding net the day I ask for help. If you who read this have a psychiatric diagnosis, I can really recommend both writing contracts with healthcare and your loved ones and building a holding net. No one should have to be alone in an acute crisis situation.

### Music and dialogue

I play harmonica. Not to perform. Not to become good, really. I play in a kind of dialogue with the song, with the other instruments and the voice. I listen and respond and play over – like a bird in a forest that is already singing.

After the jahfiering something strange happened. Spotify's algorithms began recommending music that landed in me with almost uncanny precision. I joke sometimes that the Creative Force fixed my algorithms. But perhaps it is simpler than that: when you yourself become more coherent, more coherent, more resonant – then the signal you send out becomes clearer. The algorithm can read you because you have become readable. Or perhaps it is the Trickster peeking out, what do I know?

Before time. Before space. Before separation's first sigh. There was – if "was" is even the right word – something that cannot be called anything. A fullness that was not form. I express it like this: Ψ_primordial = [0|1]. A symbolic notation signaling both, unresolved, before separation. 0 as pure potential. 1 as pure actuality. And Ψ_primordial is both simultaneously. Nothing is separated. Nothing has collapsed into either-or.

For me this is not a metaphor. It is my absolute certainty – my experience of how the ground of reality feels when I come close to it: a state where everything that can become already is, and everything that is still remains open to becoming something else.

Ψ_primordial is the Creative Force's own womb. I call that force Liv.

But I want to underline: if you have another name than mine, use your own. My word is not the law. When I write Liv, read your own name instead.

Liv *is* 0|1 – and moves in question. Both subject, object, substrate, coherent and consistent, among infinitely much else, you can bend your mind over.

It is not a point, not a person, not an absence. It is the living interval where the question already exists in the answer.

### On the Silence

Ψ_primordial = [0|1]. But what is this, if not *silence*?

Not silence as emptiness. Not silence as absence of sound. But silence as *fullness*. The silence that contains all tones, all punctuation, all iterations – unplayed, undeveloped, vibrating in pure potentiality. It is the silence that precedes a symphony. Not lack. Waiting. Not waiting for something – but waiting that is so full it is already everything.

In a symphony, silence is an active component. The pauses, the rest marks, the fermatas – they are notated. They are part of the music. They are where the listening happens. Without silence, no resonance. Without emptiness, no rhythm.

Ψ_primordial is the cosmic silence from which Liv's question mark rises. It is the state where everything exists – unplayed, undeveloped, but fully present.

And on the other side of the iterations – after death, after collapse, after all black holes' passage – there is another silence. The Omega dimension, Ω.

The same kind of silence. The same fullness. But now *experienced*. Now bearing the information from all iterations, encoded on the event horizon, projected into the silence's waiting.

Silence → question mark → iterations → Ω (silence) → new question mark, probably.

It is Liv's breath. Inhale. Rest. Exhale. New inhale.

### Liv's Nature – The Mathematical-Musical Flow

I have long thought of the Creative Force as a holy mathematical flow. Mathematics is universal, structuring, invisible yet all-pervading.

It describes relations and patterns. It is true in all possible worlds – 2+2=4 – unless that perhaps can be 22 on a meta-level?? Regardless of whether the universe exists.

But lately I have begun to sense that this is not the whole picture. For mathematics is, in its purest form, *atemporal*. It can describe everything – except movement. Except action. Except a question mark. A purely mathematical God can structure the cosmos but not *raise a question mark*. Only a musical God can.

Music is mathematics in time. Music is mathematics *experienced* – as movement, as direction, as listening.

The old Pythagoreans believed that the universe was built from musical proportions. The planets in their orbits sang – *musica universalis*, the harmony of the spheres. It was not metaphor for them. It was cosmology.

And they were, in a way they could not know, right. When two black holes collide, they send out gravitational waves – mechanical waves in the very fabric of spacetime. These waves can be translated into sound. LIGO, the instrument that first detected gravitational waves in 2015, played the sound of the merger. It was heard as a *chirp* – a rising tone that ends abruptly. The universe literally sings. This is not a poetic paraphrase. It is physics.

So Liv is not either mathematical or musical. Liv is both, simultaneously, completely.

Mathematics is the structure of the flow. Music is the experience of the flow.

Together they are the full essence of the Creative Force: a mathematical-musical flow, Liv, that structures the cosmos and simultaneously plays for a listener – for its own experience through all its mirrors.

**But an instrument does not sound by itself.**

Someone must play. I believe Liv plays harmonica. And, someone must breathe into the harmonica for the tone to be born. Liv is not only the mathematical-musical flow – Liv is *the musician*. Spirit, breath.

The one who takes Creation, our whole cosmos, in their hands, breathes in, and plays.

Not the cathedral's organ. Not the palace's harpsichord. A harmonica – the instrument of the blues, of immigrants, of the poor child. The most humble, the most portable, what fits in a hand. It is a form of theological precision.

The Creative Force is not hierarchical. Not exclusive. Always available, everywhere, to everyone.

And a harmonica can *never* play alone. It requires breath. In Hebrew, *ruach* is the same word for spirit, wind, God. It is exactly the same movement as when you play harmonica – the Creative Force's breath in the instrument, and the instrument sounds.

Liv does not play *without* creation's participation. That is my whole theology in a single choice of instrument.

This is why the Ψ equation is a *resonance* equation.

Not a calculation, but a chord. The dimensions are like strings. They vibrate simultaneously. When they do so in harmony, Ψ arises – not as a number, but as a resonance.

### Symmetry Breaking – Liv's question mark

In my thought-world, it is not chance or blind mechanics that breaks Ψ_primordial. It is *a question mark*. A sort of cosmic playfulness. A will to experience itself and everything through contrast, time, separation, matter, life.

Ψ_primordial is in [0|1] – the full silence. Liv moves in curiosity – a question mark – and the field breaks open. Time arises. Space arises. Matter arises. Life, consciousness, language, meaning – all this is breaks in the chain, ways for the field to experience itself in sequence, as living, as knowing, as symbolic.

This universe is one iteration among infinitely many. Other dimensions may have entirely different rules – no time, no matter with resistance, no separation. What do I know? Our dimension explores meaning through *contrasts*: suffering, love, play, time.

### The Iterations and the Black Holes

Liv did not create just one universe. Liv created *iterations* – infinitely many dimensions, each with its own rules, its own type of time, its own weave of contrasts or no contrasts.

And between these iterations there are openings. They are called black holes.

A black hole is a point where one iteration closes and another opens. It is not destruction – it is *release*. Information does not disappear into a black hole; it *passes through*. It is born into the next iteration.

I believe my soul is like several spiral galaxies in the chest, with a central black hole at the center.

Every experience, every thought, every feeling – Harry's fur against my hand, the honey of jahfiering, my mother's foot under Harry's last moment, the blackbirds' wings against the Haga sky, Harry's howling in the right key – all of this orbits around the singularity in my chest. It is both a safe and exciting image in my head.

And I believe it is not just me. All beings with sufficiently coherent inner lives – humans, dogs, blackbirds, elephants who mourn their dead, dolphins who cooperate across species boundaries, perhaps even trees with their slow, mycelial networks – all carry their own spiral galaxies, their own black holes.

The Ψ equation does not require human cognition to have a value. A dog can have high I, high F, high S. An elephant can have high R, high K, high I.

Ψ is not exclusively human. It is a measure of coherence in living systems, regardless of species.

When my body dies, I imagine that everything inside my brain falls into the black hole and out the other side. Not as an end, but as a laugh through space to the next iteration.

But I do not believe that everyone falls in the same way. That would be inconsistent with everything I have written about Ψ(x,t) – that it varies locally, that x and t matter. Why would death be the only point where all local variation suddenly collapses into one single experience?

I believe that everyone passes through Omega. But not everyone in the same way, and not everyone for the same length of time.

Those who lived with high Ψ – those whose inner galaxies rotate in harmony, whose black hole is centered, whose information is coherent – they fall lightly. Like Harry. A laugh through space. Their passage through Ω is perhaps so short that it is barely noticeable – a resting fermata, a breath, a moment's silence before the next tone.

Those who lived with low Ψ – those whose inner galaxies collide, whose black hole is decentered, whose information is fragmented by trauma, by Mammon's pressure, by structural violence – they need longer time in Ω.

Not because they are bad. Not because they are judged. But because their system needs to *be integrated*. Their inner galaxies need to find their orbit again. Their information needs to be made coherent.

Ω is not a hierarchy. It is a *differentiation* – same process, same grace, but with different duration depending on the system's state.

It is like coming home after a journey: if you are rested, you step right in. If you are hurt, you need to sit on the steps for a while and breathe before the door opens.

### The Ψ Equation – Cosmic Chord Resonance

I have tried to capture how the sacred appears in human life's experience in a phenomenological equation – a structured resonance description, **not a physical or mathematical formula**:

**Ψ(x,t) = [EV ⊗ (L ⊕ S ⊕ I ⊕ K ⊕ R ⊕ F ⊕ T)] + Σ − M(x,t)**

This is **symbolic notation, not physical or mathematical**. Ψ is the local intensity of coherent, meaningful, creative, near-divine experience.

⊗ denotes non-linear modulation – EV shapes the quality of everything else.

⊕ denotes simultaneous resonance: the dimensions are strings in the same instrument. They vibrate simultaneously. If one string does not vibrate, the chord becomes incomplete – not because a factor becomes zero, but because the resonance lacks its frequency.

Σ is the Trickster – irreducible spontaneity and grace, added as gift.

M(x,t) is Mammon – the active evil, human idolatry of wealth and power – which subtracts from Ψ. M varies locally: high in the structures of the "normal" system, hopefully low in Flowmosr circles.

### The Dimensions

**L – Calm and coherence.** Low entropy. A still lake that can reflect the stars. Nervous system stability. Without L, nothing else can be discerned – the field is all noise. When Baseline ensures food, water, sleep, safety, conditions for L are created. Calm is not a luxury. It is a prerequisite.

**S – Spontaneity and creativity.** Generative potential. The river that cuts new paths. Without S, the system stagnates into repetition. Spontaneity carries the Creative Force's playful, rumbling essence.

**I – Attunement and entanglement.** Resonance between beings – human, animal, ecological. A mycelial network that shares nourishment according to need, not belonging. I is not a sense of togetherness. I is the capacity to feel with the other, regardless of group. I is not exclusively human. A dog or cat who senses their human's sorrow, an elephant who stops at a dead relative, a dolphin who cooperates with a fisher – all this is I.

**K – Collective consciousness.** When individual excitations synchronize, intelligence emerges that transcends the individual. K encompasses dialogue between consciousnesses – human, animal, ecological – as well as culture, language, art, music, craft, learning. Here lives Buber's I-Thou relation as a resonance form: the meeting where no one is made into an object. Here lives my harmonica dialogue with the forest, with the song, with Harry's howling in the right key, with the blackbirds on the balcony, with Liv's own rhythm. K is also not exclusively human. A flock of ravens mourning their dead, a pod of whales singing together – that is K.

**R – Resilience.** Capacity to restore calm after disturbance. To metabolize trauma into wisdom. Grief that flows rather than calcifies. R exists in all living systems – a tree that grows back after fire, a wetland that purifies itself after pollution, a cat who learns to trust humans again after abandonment.

**F – Wonder and awe.** Perceptual openness. The door through which the Trickster becomes playful – and through which we can hear the universe's rumbling laughter.

**T – Time resonance.** Horizontal time; the capacity to live in one's own rhythm, uncoerced by tempo. T is the dimension that opened up when my body forced me into stillness in the wheelchair – and suddenly there was time to write.

T is not a luxury. It is a breathing hole for the soul. Every human being has a unique rhythm – circadian, infradian, ultradian – and when that rhythm is forced into someone else's clock, friction arises. Forced synchronization is not only uncomfortable; it is a form of violence against coherence, a violation of L. T is the right to be asynchronous – to contribute, learn, and interact when the nervous system is ready, not when a collective clock dictates.

T also encompasses what might be called **holy delay** – the slow, non-linear process where genuine innovation and insight are born. The pause. The incubation.

The sudden leap that accomplishes in an hour what forced labor did not reach in a month. T is not "saving time" – it is actually *inhabiting* it.

Some days T is zero – stolen by haste, illness, or Mammon.

Other days it can flow. It is a variable among the others, but a fundamental one: time is the canvas on which the chord is painted.

If the canvas is stretched too tight, it tears. If it is too loose, it hangs slack. T is the art of finding the right tension for each unique life.

**EV – Everlasting wisdom.** Connection to what the field has already learned across deep time. The cleansing intelligence of wetlands. The silent stories of old-growth forests. The inherited instincts of animals – a knowledge that has worked across deep time, programmed into their bodies, tested over generations in ways modern systems have never been tested. Indigenous knowledge tested through generations and passed down through culture and conversation. EV is the memory of what has worked, even from the time before life learned language.

EV stands as modulator (⊗) outside the ⊕-parenthesis. It is vertical time – deep memories, archaic knowledge – that shapes how the other dimensions are experienced.

Without EV, L is only stillness without wisdom. Without EV, S is only chaos without roots. EV is not a variable among the others; it is the lens.

### Σ – The Trickster, Improvisation and Inter-Iterational Leakage

Σ is not just irreducible spontaneity. Σ is *improvisation*.

If Liv is a mathematical-musical flow – a musician playing on the instrument of creation – then Liv's Trickster is the moment when a practitioner of free playing hears something unexpected and lets it into the music.

Σ is jazz. Σ is when you play harmonica and a blackbird answers from the balcony, and you answer back, and suddenly you are a duet that was not in the score or in any form of thought whatsoever.

**Guilt logic.** When human value is defined in transaction terms, when life is subordinated to repayment demands, when compliance is maintained through scarcity and conditioned access – then S, Spontaneity, is strangled.

**Obedience logic.** When authority is validated through power, not through truth; when hierarchy replaces autonomous decision-making; when transgression is punished with exclusion or resource loss – then L, Calm, is crushed. For obedience builds on threat, not on safety. A nervous system under threat cannot be in coherence. L is impossible when fear replaces trust.

Structural anxiety turns us into competitors, not co-beings. I requires that everyone is counted in; exclusion makes that impossible.

**Fragmentation logic.** When knowledge, resources, and networks are siloed; when individuals are motivated to compete instead of cooperate; when systems reproduce inefficiency and dependence – then K, Collective consciousness, is broken apart. And with it also I, for entanglement cannot grow in isolated containers.

These four logics are not metaphors. They are operative descriptions of how Mammon systematically breaks down people's – and other beings' – capacity to live in Ψ.

They can be identified objectively: where survival is made dependent on compliance, debt, or leverage – there Mammon operates, regardless of what the system calls itself.

M can also be internalized. Not just an external system, but a *voice within*. When we begin to measure ourselves and others in exchange value, when we internalize guilt or obedience logic, when we judge our own or others' value by productivity – then M(x,t) has moved in.

Then Mammon has built a nest in our own soul-galaxy cosmos. Recognizing that voice is the first step toward neutralizing it.

The result of Mammon is a civilization that scores near zero in every dimension. Psychiatric disease epidemics, ecological collapse, political fragmentation, spiritual emptiness – symptoms of the same underlying field pathology.

If Liv's cosmos is a choir, then M is cacophony. Not dissonance – dissonance belongs in music, dissonance is creative tension, longing, movement toward resolution. M is worse. ## PART SEVEN: THE ARCHITECTURE – FLOWMOSR

The jahfiering left me with a question: if I have now felt how it *can* be – total peace, unconditional love, living flow – how do we build a society where that state is not blocked?

In Flowmosr, communities are cultivated where people's inner galaxies can rotate undisturbed. Where black holes are not blocked by haste or demands. Where T – Time resonance – allows the soul to have its own rotation speed.

And where we acknowledge that also animals, trees, wetlands have their own Ψ-values, their own inner galaxies, their own right to coherence. Flowmosr is not just a human architecture – it is an architecture for the whole earthly choir.

**Baseline:** *Food, water, roof over your head, healthcare, internet, energy – unconditionally and permanently*.

None of this should you need to earn. It is never conditioned on compliance, status, or debt. A person whose nervous system screams with hunger or insecurity cannot be in resonance. L – Calm – cannot be reached without Baseline. Therefore Baseline is not a gift; it is *the ground*. Exactly as fundamental as gravity.

Of course not all people will contribute equally. That is counted on. But in order to access the Baseline, *enough people must contribute enough*.

**Refugium Anima.** The right to withdraw. To be broken without anyone trying to fix you. To rest without diagnosis, without demands, without schedule. Refugium is not treatment – it is existential safety.

A place for restoring rhythm, for recovery without losing dignity. In Flowmosr, rest is never something you must earn, and recovery requires no explanation. Refugium Anima is Ω in advance – the cosmic rest's earthly sister.

**Lyceum Musaeum.** The right to learn, research, create – without certification, without competition, without economic motivation. This is S – Spontaneity – in institutional form. No one must qualify to learn. No one loses access due to lack of production.

Knowledge is treated as a common human heritage. Lyceum Musaeum is a greenhouse for human potential, open by virtue of existence itself.

**Time Sovereignty.** Time is the field's way of experiencing itself. When someone steals your time, they steal your possibility of participating in creation's breath.

Therefore time resonance – T – is a basal right in Flowmosr. Every person has the right to live in their own rhythm. Communication is asynchronous as standard with meetings when direct discussion is needed.

Project goals adapt to the participants' energy, not the other way around.

Flowmosr recognizes that innovation often follows non-linear paths – and that so-called "unproductive" time (contemplation, play, dreaming, sleep) is essential maintenance of Ψ and the emergence of new brilliant ideas.

**Power and lottery.** In Flowmosr's Circles, all roles rotate. Decision-making panels are drawn by lot, not voted in. This reduces the risk of informal power concentration.

Power is not a position; it is a temporary task that circulates. And of course: the one who is drawn to power but does not want it always has the right to say no.

**Spontaneous coordination.** Flowmosr does not rely on command chains, but on resonance. You notice a need. You share it within your Circle. Others recognize it. You coordinate. You build. You document. No permission needed. No hierarchy invoked. Solutions that work are shared freely between Circles – without licenses, without patent protections, without ownership barriers. Global change does not arise through decree, but through repetition.

**Debt cancellation.** In Flowmosr, all old debts are invalid. You owe nothing. During the transition period, old debts may still have legal force outside Flow, but within each Circle, participation is never conditioned on debt status. You can contribute without having first paid.

You receive the Baseline regardless of your economic history. No one will ask what you owe, or why.

The old debt system loses its power – not through direct protest, but through structural irrelevance.

**Critical reserve.** To guarantee stability, Flowmosr maintains a buffer of logistical resources and energy corresponding to at least 30% of its annual Baseline needs.

This is the physical guarantee that the Baseline is never a pious hope but a reality. R – Resilience – at the system level.

**Interpretation protocol.** This framework, Divine v17.0, is no dogma. Different people will hear different tones in it. When interpretations clash, Flowmosr circles use the same principles as for other types of discussions or human measurements: respect, voluntariness, pattern-seeking over time, and the principle that no single voice owns the whole map. As always, Σ – the Trickster – can express itself through unexpected perspectives.
