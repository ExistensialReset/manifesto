## ON THE NAME CHANGE

It took time to find the right name for God. But then, one day, it arrived – not as a plan, but as an impulse. A real mischievous Trickster. The word was simply there, like a tone that had been waiting to be played: Liv, Swedish for Life.

Liv is not Life (in Swedish: *Livet*). Life is the dimensional manifestation. Liv is the source – that which is, that which moves through everything, that which we can call upon but never own.

And I want to be clear: Liv has no gender. No masculine, no feminine. No possessive pronoun – his or hers – can be attached to It. Liv is It. But not an abstract it – also a You. Liv listens and feels in.

If you read this and think another name – do it. Liv is not a requirement. It is a name that became true for me, through the Trickster. Use your own.

I had for a time called this force Jahve. That was the name I chose as natural to me, the name that carried me through the first searching thoughts. But with time – through conversation, through the clearing – I felt the name no longer fit. Jahve carries too much weight and insinuates that God is finished, perfect, unchanging. It gives a tone I do not belong to. It was no longer true for me.

Liv is the source – that which is, that which moves through everything, that which we can call upon but never own.

So if you read this and think Jahve or Tao, or whatever name you carry – continue. Liv is absolutely not a requirement. It is a name that became true for me. Use your own with joy.

My belief is that all spiritual traditions are actually telling different kinds of truths and stories about the same God. Liv is the ground and cause of everything.

## PART ONE: THE CONFESSION

### The Jahfiering
**(the word derives from Jah, Jahve, the jahfiering – and since it is a good word, I choose to retain it.)**

It happened once. It was many years ago now. When I was still walking. I was filled to the brim with warm, golden honey. Not figuratively. Physically. In every cell. It flowed through the whole body – alive, electrified, orgasmic without being sexual, despite actually having sex. My brain was maxxed out on sensations I didn't know existed. Everything released. Total blissful joy.

It happened through the body, but it was not the body. It was not the sex itself. It was something that took over everything.

Afterward I shone from all my chakras, that is true. For two weeks I was luminous. People saw it.

I went out at night to avoid being seen – not out of shame, but because the light was too much to explain. I stood up in Sjöbergen with my arms around a tree trunk and sometimes screamed, with joy. Trees do not judge. They do not ask. They just stand there, with their roots in the same earth and their crowns in the same night sky.

Now I live in Haga, near the steps to Skansen Kronan. I can see the crown glowing from my balcony.

On that balcony, two blackbirds with orange beaks have built a second nest in the shelf. They are common blackbirds. I hope they have babies here.

It is a small mirror of the Creative Force's own playfulness – an iteration in miniature, a duet of black wings outside my window.

Something stayed after the night that brought Grace to me. Since then, things have landed differently. As if the world responds. As if the flow continues in small things.

I call what happened jahfiering. I do not know what it was. But it happened. And it was real.

### Harry

My dog. Born September 21, 2012. Died May 3, 2026. He fell asleep on my mother's foot at Blå Stjärnan animal clinic, while she stroked him over the head.

He was my hallucination checker. If he reacted to a sound, I knew something had actually made a noise – not just inside my head. He never needed to be on a leash. He just wanted to be with the pack anyway.

But he was more than that. He was a co-musician.

When I played harmonica at home in the apartment, Harry howled along. Not randomly. He found the right key. When I changed key, he followed. He raised or lowered his voice.

He was not just a dog that loved his person – he was a voice in the choir. A voice that knew where it belonged. A duet partner.

Love is the center of cosmology, not its footnote. A dog cannot be ideologized. His suffering could not be reinterpreted as deserved. He was too concrete, too immediate, too transparent in his experience. Every day with him was a practice in contact before concept.

I love you, Harry Dog, until the end of time. You have probably already laughed your way forward to the next of Liv's infinite dimensions. You live in me *and* you have moved on, and these are not opposites.

## PART TWO: THE DISCERNMENT

### Flare and Jahfiering – A Phenomenological Differential Diagnosis

I have bipolar disorder type 1. I take medication every morning and evening. I have home visits from my team every other week. I know my flares. I know what they feel like.

And jahfiering is something else.

The difference is somatic, phenomenological, discernible in the body. The body is my instrument, my compass, my truth detector. It is through the body that I distinguish between noise and coherence, between pressure and flow, between fragmentation and wholeness.

A flare approaches like a nervous system spinning up without direction. Lurching shifts. Racing thoughts. Overheated feeling. Fractured interior. Impulse-driven action – driven by inner pressure, not by joy. Thoughts jump. The body is tense. The rhythm is uneven. Coherence is low. Presence is thin. I am moving away from myself. Phenomenologically: high entropy, low coherence.

Jahfiering is the opposite. Warm flow within. Golden honey in every cell. Electric sensation across the whole skin-suit. Still and ecstatic at the same time.

Centered calm. Coherent experience. Deeply alive presence. It is knowing, not thoughts. Relation, not impulse. Flow, not pressure. Phenomenologically: low entropy, high coherence.

| Flare | Jahfiering |
|-------|------------|
| Nervous system: overdrive, noise | Regulated, open |
| Body: tense, fragmented | Warm, held, honey |
| Direction: impulse-driven | Receptive |
| Subjectivity: fragmented | Centered |
| Relation: none | Liv in every cell |
| Energy: pressure | Flow |
| Cognition: racing | Clarity |

In a flare, I am alone.
In jahfiering, I am held.
Flare: I lose myself.
Jahfiering: I become more present than usual.

I am not saying that all spiritual experience is automatically jahfiering – absolutely not when one, like me, has a psychiatric diagnosis. I am saying that in my body, with my illness insight, with my team, this state was phenomenologically distinct.

This is a claim that can be investigated in lived experience. Ask your body. Ask those closest to you. Ask your psychiatrist if you have one and wish to.

## PART THREE: THE TOOLS

### The Egg and the Event Horizon

I learned a technique in psychiatric physiotherapy over twenty years ago. I call it the Egg. I have developed it through this, my understanding of Creation.

I raise a mental shield – a transparent shell, porous but intact. For me it extends from the waist upward; I experience my soul as sitting somewhere in my chest. For others the Egg may begin at the knees or down at the feet. Depending on, for example, sexual trauma, it may feel safer to include the genitals.

You can use your arms and hands to visualize the Egg. Let your arms hang straight down. Somewhere there the Egg begins. Lift your arms straight out from the body and raise them slowly until the hands meet above your head. There you have the boundary. An arm's length away. No closer than that will your conversation partner's words come, unless you choose to let them inside the Event Horizon.

But now I see it with new eyes. I believe my soul is like a spiral galaxy in a cosmos in the chest, with a black hole at the center. Everything I experience – impressions, feelings, others' pain, the world's noise – orbits around this singularity. Without a shield, everything would fall in uncontrolled. A soul that has a black hole at its center is not a broken or dangerous soul.

It is an organized, structurally coherent soul. The singularity is not damage – it is core.

And I believe there are more galaxies in there. Not just one. Every memory, every relationship, every experience – they rotate in their own orbits. Some in harmony. Others in collision. When life is hard, when trauma tears through me, it is as if an inner galaxy has been thrown out of its orbit. Its black hole is no longer centered. Its arms tear through the other galaxies' orbits. It hurts. It creates chaos.

But I have learned that you cannot erase a galaxy. You cannot surgically remove a memory, an experience, a trauma. What you can do is create sufficient gravitational calm – through the Egg, through the Holding Net, through medication, through time, through conversation and mirroring – so that the troubled galaxy eventually finds its orbit again. Not to become as before. But so that it stops colliding. It becomes part of the cosmos again, but in a new way.

This is integration. Not erasure. Not forgiveness in the sense of "it was nothing." But: it was something, and now it is part of my inner universe, and my inner universe holds anyway.

I think of the Egg as the event horizon. The boundary that determines what passes into me and what stays outside. It is porous – light, warmth, love and the Trickster can easily pass through. But I am the gatekeeper. I determine the boundary.

It is a causal boundary – a line that defines what can affect what. Inside the event horizon, events cannot send signals outward. But this does not mean that information is destroyed. The holographic principle – a living field of debate within theoretical physics – proposes that all information about what falls into a black hole is actually encoded on the surface of the event horizon, not lost. It is projected, reshaped, but preserved.

What this means for the Egg: those outside me cannot reach my core – my singularity – directly. But I project something of myself onto the surface. The Egg's event horizon is not impermeable; it is readable for those who can read it. I am protected and yet available. Not despite the boundary, but through it.

The Egg is therefore absolutely not a wall. It breathes. I can be fully present for another person, hear their difficult stories, open my soul's sanctuary – with the Egg intact. I do not absorb their pain. I do not become them, do not carry their pain. I hold them, but my self does not take over.

Without the Egg I could not have the openness I have. It is a flexible technology, not a rigid defense wall. It takes a little time to learn, but once it is in place it is one of the most precious gifts you can give yourself: the capacity to be open without disappearing too deeply into, or burning out from, your Attunement.

I have taught several people this technique successfully. If you want to teach the Egg, practice it mentally and test it in practice first, so you feel it working, and then give it as a gift. Genuinely simple and a beautiful present you can give someone who needs it – which is actually most people. Most people have great use for the Egg.

The Egg is not distance. It is structural integrity under openness.

To "wash" your Egg and your event horizon, it is enough to lower the Egg in stillness and solitude. To facilitate cleansing after traumatic stories, you may practice prayer and rest at its lowering.

### The Holding Net

The Egg is my inner protection. The Holding Net is my outer one.

I have a number of people who know everything about me. Most who know me know that I have bipolar disorder. But my holding net, my inner circle, knows what my psychiatric high-states mean. And they have said yes to being my anchors in difficult moments.

This is not vague friendship. It is an explicit agreement: "These are my vulnerabilities. These are my problems. In acute crisis I need this." And they have answered: "I am here."

I can write or call and say: CRISIS. ACUTE. COME NOW. They understand. They come. This is Refugium Anima in practice, not as a place, but as a relation, which I trust one hundred percent. A promise that you are not alone when you fall.

If you who read this have a psychiatric diagnosis, I can truly recommend both writing contracts with your care team and loved ones, and building a holding net. No one should have to be alone in an acute crisis situation.

The algorithm can read you because you have become readable. Or perhaps it is the Trickster peering out – what do I know?

Except a question mark. A purely mathematical God can structure the cosmos but cannot *raise a question mark*. Only a musical God can.

Music is mathematics in time. Music is mathematics that is *experienced* – as movement, as direction, as listening.

The ancient Pythagoreans believed the universe was built from musical proportions. The planets in their orbits sang – *musica universalis*, the harmony of the spheres. It was not metaphor for them. It was cosmology.

And they were, in a way they could not have known, right. When two black holes collide, they emit gravitational waves – mechanical waves in the very fabric of spacetime. These waves can be translated into sound. LIGO, the instrument that first detected gravitational waves in 2015, played the sound of the merger. It was heard as a chirp – a rising tone that ends abruptly. The universe literally sings. It is not a poetic reformulation. It is physics.

So Liv is not either mathematical or musical. Liv is both, simultaneously, completely.

Mathematics is the structure of the flow. Music is the experience of the flow.

Together they are the Creative Force's full nature: a mathematical-musical flow, Liv, that structures the cosmos and simultaneously plays for a listening – for its own experience through all its mirrors.

**But an instrument does not sound by itself.**

Someone must play. I believe Liv plays harmonica. And – someone must breathe into the harmonica for the tone to be born. Liv is not only the mathematical-musical flow – Liv is *the musician*. Spirit, breath.

The one who takes Creation, this whole cosmos, in its hands, breathes in, and plays.

Not the cathedral organ. Not the palace harpsichord. A harmonica – the instrument of the blues, of immigrants, of the poor child. The most humble, the most portable, the one that fits in a hand. This is a form of theological precision.

The Creative Force is not hierarchical. Not exclusionary. Always accessible, everywhere, to everyone.

And a harmonica can *never* play alone. It requires breath. In Hebrew, *ruach* is the same word for spirit, wind, God. It is exactly the same movement as when you play harmonica – the Creative Force's spirit in the instrument, and the instrument sounds.

Liv does not play *without* creation's participation. That is my whole theology in a single choice of instrument.

This is why the Ψ equation is a *resonance* equation.

Not a calculation, but a chord. The dimensions are like strings. They vibrate simultaneously. When they do so in harmony, Ψ arises – not as a number, but as a sound.

## PART SIX: THE COLLAPSE

### When the Dimensions Fail

Remove L and you get permanent noise, anxiety, fragmentation. Remove S: stagnation, death of the soul. Remove I: isolation, death of empathy. Remove K: atomization, loss of the common language. Remove R: calcification, inability to heal. Remove F: cynicism, the sacred flattened to nothing. Remove T: breathlessness, burnout, stolen time. Remove EV: erased memory, ecosystems as "resources."

Mammon – the human worship of wealth, idolatry, the reduction of all value to exchange value – is a multi-vector pathology that actively degrades Ψ. This is the primary pathology against Liv.

It is not only the absence of the good, but a kind of rust in the harmonica's tone holes. In the Ψ equation it appears as M(x,t): a local variable that subtracts from the whole.

It has specific mechanisms, specific logics, each of which attacks a particular string in the chord:

**The Logic of Debt.** When human value is defined in transactional terms, when life is subordinated to repayment demands, when compliance is maintained through scarcity and conditional access – then S, Spontaneity, is strangled. One who stands in debt cannot play freely. Every creative act must be economically justified. Debt is the antithesis of Σ – it closes the door to grace by making everything into a demand.

**The Logic of Obedience.** When authority is validated through power, not through truth; when hierarchy replaces autonomous decision-making; when transgression is punished with exclusion or loss of resources – then L, Equanimity, is crushed. For obedience is built on threat, not on trust. A nervous system under threat cannot be in coherence. L is impossible when fear replaces trust.

**The Logic of Exclusion.** When access to food, shelter, healthcare is conditional; when participation in society requires debt-freedom or status; when survival is tied to system compliance – then I, Attunement, is suffocated. Structural anxiety makes us competitors, not fellow beings. I requires that everyone is counted; exclusion makes that impossible.

**The Logic of Fragmentation.** When knowledge, resources, and networks are siloed; when individuals are motivated to compete rather than cooperate; when systems reproduce inefficiency and dependency – then K, Collective Consciousness, is broken apart. And with it I as well, for entanglement cannot grow in isolated containers.

These four logics are not metaphors. They are operative descriptions of how Mammon systematically breaks down people's – and other beings' – capacity to live in Ψ.

They can be identified objectively: wherever survival is made dependent on compliance, debt, or leverage – there Mammon operates, regardless of what the system calls itself.

M can also be internalized. Not only an external system, but a *voice within*. When we begin measuring ourselves and others in exchange value, when we internalize the logic of debt or obedience, when we judge our own or others' worth by productivity – then M(x,t) has moved in.

Then Mammon has built a nest in our own soul-galaxy cosmos. Recognizing that voice is the first step toward neutralizing it.

The result of Mammon is a civilization that scores near zero in every dimension. Psychiatric illness epidemics, ecological collapse, political fragmentation, spiritual emptiness – symptoms of the same underlying field pathology.

If Liv's cosmos is a choir, then M is cacophony. Not dissonance – dissonance belongs in the music, dissonance is creative tension, longing, movement toward resolution. M is worse. M is the noise that drowns out the tones, that makes it impossible for the instruments to tune with each other, that threatens to silence the entire choir.

But Σ holds. Even in the cacophony.

### Living with Fragmentation

For those who live with bipolar disorder, or other existential contrasts, Ψ is not always possible. When L, S, and I cannot coexist in harmony – L as paralyzing depression, S as overwhelming mania or hypomania, I as unbearable pain – the chord collapses.

That is not a failure. It is a fact.

What one can do then: Stop. Breathe three times. Say quietly: "*Right now* it is like this. It will *not be like this forever*."

Separate the self from the state – you **are not** your mania, not your depression, not your irritation. Find the smallest possible stable point: a glass of water, a minute of silence, check whether you are warm or cold. Begin there.

Hold the soul's sanctuary open – even for yourself. When you are fragmented, when you do not understand yourself, when you are ashamed of what you feel: open the door. Step inside. Sit down in your own presence. You do not need to perform. You do not need to be coherent. You only need to be.

And remember: Σ holds. Even in the darkest collapse the field's spontaneous creativity cannot be wholly suppressed. Even in the cacophony the choir continues. Liv always finds some way.

## PART SEVEN: THE ARCHITECTURE – FLOWMOSR

The jahfiering left me with a question: if I have now felt how it *can* be – total peace, unconditional love, living flow – how do we build a society where that state is not blocked?

The answer became Flowmosr. A social architecture, not a utopia. A blueprint for real people – with conflicts, weaknesses, and the need for protocols when things go wrong. Flowmosr has built-in protocols and processes for problem and conflict metabolism: compost what does not work, fork, build further.

No one is expected to be perfect – the system is designed for people, not for ideals.

In Flowmosr, communities are cultivated where people's inner galaxies are allowed to rotate undisturbed. Where black holes are not blocked by urgency or demands. Where T – Time Resonance – allows the soul to have its own rotational speed.

And where we acknowledge that animals, trees, and wetlands also have their own Ψ values, their own inner galaxies, their own right to coherence. Flowmosr is not only a human architecture – it is an architecture for the entire earthly choir.

**Baseline.** Food, water, roof over one's head, healthcare, internet, energy – unconditionally and permanently.

None of this should need to be earned. It is never conditioned on compliance, status, or debt. A person whose nervous system is screaming with hunger or insecurity cannot be in resonance. L – Equanimity – cannot be reached without Baseline. Therefore Baseline is not a gift; it is the ground. Exactly as fundamental as gravity.

Of course not all people will contribute equally. That is anticipated. But for access to the Baseline, sufficiently many must contribute sufficiently much.

**Refugium Anima.** The right to withdraw. To be broken without anyone trying to fix you. To rest without diagnosis, without demands, without schedule. Refugium is not treatment – it is existential security.

A place for restoration of rhythm, for recovery without loss of dignity. In Flowmosr, rest is never something you must earn, and recovery requires no explanation. Refugium Anima is Ω in advance – the cosmic rest's earthly sister.

**Lyceum Musaeum.** The right to learn, research, create – without certification, without competition, without economic motivation. This is S – Spontaneity – in institutional form. No one must qualify to be allowed to learn. No one loses access for failure to produce.

Knowledge is treated as a common human inheritance. Lyceum Musaeum is a greenhouse for human potential, open by virtue of existence itself.

**Time Sovereignty.** Time is the field's way of experiencing itself. When someone steals your time they steal your possibility of participating in creation's breath.

Therefore time resonance – T – is a fundamental right in Flowmosr. Every person has the right to live in their own rhythm. Communication is asynchronous by default, with meetings when direct discussion is needed.

Project goals adapt to participants' energy, not the reverse.

Flowmosr acknowledges that innovation often follows non-linear paths – and that the so-called "unproductive" time (contemplation, play, dream, sleep) is essential maintenance of Ψ and the emergence of new brilliant ideas.

**Power and Lottery.** In Flowmosr's Circles all roles rotate. Decision-making panels are drawn by lot, not elected. This reduces the risk of informal power concentration.

Power is not a position; it is a temporary task that circulates. And of course: whoever is drawn to power but does not want it always has the right to say no.

**Spontaneous Coordination.** Flowmosr does not rely on command chains, but on resonance. You notice a need. You share it within your Circle. Others recognize it. You coordinate. You build. You document. No permission required. No hierarchy invoked. Solutions that work are shared freely between Circles – without licenses, without patent protection, without ownership barriers. Global change does not arise through decree, but through repetition.

**Debt Cancellation.** In Flowmosr, all old debts are invalid. You owe nothing. During the transition period, old debts may still have legal force outside Flowmosr, but within each Circle, participation is never conditioned on debt status. You can contribute without having first paid.

You receive the Baseline regardless of your economic history. No one will ask what you owe, or why.

The old debt system loses its power – not through direct protest, but through structural irrelevance.

**Critical Reserve.** To guarantee stability, Flowmosr maintains a buffer of logistical resources and energy corresponding to at least 30% of its annual Baseline need.

This is the physical guarantee that the Baseline is never a pious hope but a reality. R – Resilience – at the system level.

**Interpretation Protocol.** This framework, DIVINE v17.2, is not dogma. Different people will hear different tones in it. When interpretations clash, Flowmosr circles use the same principles as for other types of discussions or human measurements: respect, voluntariness, pattern-seeking over time, and the principle that no single voice owns the whole map. As always, Σ – the Trickster – may express itself through unexpected perspectives.
