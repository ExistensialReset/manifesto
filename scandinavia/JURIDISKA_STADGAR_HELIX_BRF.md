# ⚖️ HELIX_COOPERATIVE_BYLAWS.md
## Governing the Living Architecture

**Version:** 1.0 (Refugium Anima Integrated)  
**Status:** LEGAL FOUNDATION  
**Principle:** L × S × I (Human sovereignty within collective care)

---

## ARTICLE 1: PURPOSE & MISSION

### 1.1 Non-Extractive Housing
The HELIX Cooperative exists to provide non-speculative, affordable, and accessible housing. The building is a commons, not a financial asset.

### 1.2 The L × S × I Mandate
All decisions must protect the balance of:
- **L (Lugn):** Right to silence, security, and the Refugium.
- **S (Spontanitet):** Right to individual autonomy and non-conformity.
- **I (Inkännande):** Responsibility for collective wellbeing and empathy.

---

## ARTICLE 2: MEMBERSHIP & RESIDENCY

### 2.1 Unit Allocation
- **29 Soul Dwellings:** Residential units for members.
- **1 Refugium Anima:** A common sanctuary (Unit 30) that cannot be converted into a residence.

### 2.2 Entry Pathways
Membership is granted via:
- **Sweat Equity:** 200 hours of contribution.
- **Solidarity Deposit:** 0 – 200,000 kr (sliding scale).
- **Temporal Path:** Deferred contribution for those in crisis.

---

## ARTICLE 3: REFUGIUM ANIMA (THE SANCTUARY)

### 3.1 Right to Retreat
Every resident has an unconditional right to use Refugium Anima. 

### 3.2 Access Protocol
- **Anonymity:** No logbooks or keys required. Access is open 24/7.
- **Non-Judgment:** Use of the Refugium is never grounds for wellness checks or intervention unless requested by the user.
- **Resource Priority:** The 7,970 kr monthly fee guarantees the maintenance of this space as a primary operational cost.

---

## ARTICLE 4: FINANCIAL GOVERNANCE

### 4.1 Fixed Monthly Cost
The monthly fee is set at **7,970 kr**. This covers:
- Loan servicing (JAK Bank).
- All utilities (Water, Heat, Energy, 1Gbps Fiber).
- 80% of food needs (The Ocean & Gardens).
- Maintenance of Refugium Anima.

### 4.2 Non-Eviction Policy
Financial hardship is a collective problem. If a resident cannot pay, the **Solidarity Reserve** (funded by Path 2 deposits) covers the cost. No resident shall be made homeless.

---

## ARTICLE 5: SOVEREIGNTY (S-FACTOR)

### 5.1 The Soul Dwelling
A resident's unit is their sovereign territory. 
- **Privacy:** No mandatory inspections.
- **Behavior:** Smoking, noise, and lifestyle choices are permitted within the unit due to >40dB acoustic isolation and HEPA-filtration.
