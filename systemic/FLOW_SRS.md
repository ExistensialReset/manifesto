## 3. ALLOCATION OF LUXURY RESOURCES
*Luxury resources are unique by nature (time, place, personhood), not by artificial scarcity.*

### 3.1 Social Recognition Score (SRS)
A non-coercive reflection of contribution, used **only** for luxury allocation.
* **Dimensions:** 
    * **L (Labor):** Logistical work.  
    * **S (Support):** Emotional/relational labor.  
    * **I (Initiative):** Creative leadership.  
    * **X (Extraordinary):** Crisis management or major donations.
* **Formula:** $SRS = L + S + I + X$
* **Decay:** Natural half-life of 1-3 months to prevent permanent elites.

### 3.2 Pay-It-Forward
SRS priority can be redirected to others. This neutralizes status fixation and rewards generosity as a high-value contribution.
