## 1. PRINCIPLES OF FORKING
Nodes may choose different AI designs based on culture, technical capability, or risk tolerance. 

* **No Forced Consensus:** No Node is obligated to adopt majority choice.
* **Knowledge Sharing:** All forks agree to share security vulnerabilities and ethical lessons.
* **Migration Rights:** Nodes may switch forks at any time without penalty.

## 2. TECHNICAL INTEROPERABILITY
All forks must maintain a **Common Standards Layer**:
* **Data Format Compatibility:** Ability to read each other's logs.
* **Security Protocols:** Shared encryption standards.
* **Baseline Monitoring:** Common metrics for $L \times S \times I$.
