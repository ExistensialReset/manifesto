## Individual Primacy Principle

The foundational unit of M-OS-R is the person.

Households may exist as living arrangements,
but they are not treated as governance units.

Every person participates in Circles as an individual.

This protects autonomy and prevents structural power from being hidden inside family structures.

Every person is YOU (DU)

## Rotation Principle

Participation in Nodes and Regional structures happens through rotation.

Individuals temporarily serve coordination roles
and then return to their Circles.

These roles do not represent permanent authority
and do not transfer decision power away from Circles.

Rotation distributes knowledge and prevents power concentration.
